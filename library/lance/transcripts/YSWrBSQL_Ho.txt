Title: Goal setting 101 🎯
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=YSWrBSQL_Ho
======================================================================

You'll be better than 90% of traders if you start 2026 by setting intentional goals. Step one to do so, review your trades. You want to break down your year by playbook. Log your top 25 trades and bottom 25 trades and find out what's driving your P&L. Step two, set stretch goals. Choose goals that push you, like sticking to your trading plan every trading session or doing maximum three trades every day if you're having issues with selectivity. But don't attach emotions to a dollar number so that you don't force trades that aren't there. Step three, you want to plan for asymmetric skewed opportunities. As traders gain experience and develop their risk management, they start to push their sizing as the setup quality improves. Step four, balance your trading with your life. You want to make sure you're getting enough sleep. You want to hang out with your friends and family and step out from the trading world for some time so that you're able to stay fresh and perform. Step five, set monthly check-ins. Goals are useless if you're not setting check-ins to your calendar so that you can review the goals and hold yourself accountable.
