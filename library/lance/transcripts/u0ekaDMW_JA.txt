The hard sad truth about trading. Only a couple percent of traders make it. Unlike many jobs where the average person can make a very solid living, the average trader loses money. For many, that's going to be discouraging. But for the couple percent that want to persevere and are not deterred by that, it really means you need to put in the work to become exceptional. And for those that do put in the work, that persevere and reach a high level of elite skills, the reward can be incredible over a multi-year career that leads to a ton of success and flexibility and is one of the most fun jobs imaginable.
