## Used by: Graham Stephan (5M Subs), Andrei Jikh, Brian Feroldi

### Overview
Systematic investment into growth ETFs using DCA. Foundation of long-term wealth building. Core + Satellite: 60-70% core DCA in ETFs, 30-40% for active swing trading.

### Core + Satellite Framework
**Core (60-70%):** VOO (40%) + QQQM (20%) — set and forget
**Satellite (30-40%):** Active swing trading using the other 24 strategies in this collection

### DCA Mechanics
- **Frequency:** Weekly ($200-500) or monthly ($1,000-2,000)
- **Amount:** Fixed dollar each period
- **Automate:** Brokerage auto-invest
- **During crashes:** BUY MORE — this is where DCA shines
- **During peaks:** Buy through the highs — don't try to time

### The Data
Vanguard study: Lump sum beats DCA 67% of the time over 10-year periods. However, DCA reduces regret risk. Best: lump sum what you have now, DCA what you earn going forward.

### Tax Strategy
- Hold > 1 year for long-term gains rates
- ETFs more tax-efficient than mutual funds
- VOO/QQQM in taxable, bonds in IRA
- Consider tax-loss harvesting

### Risk Management
- **DON'T SELL** during drawdowns (the #1 mistake)
- Rebalance annually (or when drift > 5%)
- Reduce equities as you approach goals

### Common Mistakes
1. Stopping DCA during bear markets (huge mistake)
2. Chasing last year's top performer
3. Too many overlapping ETFs
4. Trading the core (keep it separate from swing account)
