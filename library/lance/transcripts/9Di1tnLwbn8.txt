I run 24 hours a day, but only for 5 days out of the week. Not me. I run 24/7. No breaks, all gas, full-blown chaos. I'm usually pretty stable, but when huge news drops, things can move aggressively. We're very boring, but when macro data hits, I can crash way harder than you'd expect. Rookies, I don't need triggers or reasons to go ballistic. I'm volatile all the time. I'm great for people who like mixing day trades with swing trades and following company news. I'm very difficult to make money in, but at least the commissions are cheap, right? I'm best for people that thrive in chaos, fast changes, and have what it takes to run 24/7.
