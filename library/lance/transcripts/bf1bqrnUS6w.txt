You have to be committed to excellence to ever achieve high performance. And once you do that, once you commit to that excellence, then what I love to to help teach people to do and coach them to do is to build habits of excellence. One of my favorite expression expressions is from Aristotle where he he said when it came to excellence that um it's it's a habit, right? And if you can build habits of excellence, then you become a high performer. And I think that's one of the things I spend a lot of time on with traders is figuring out what habits of excellence they can build so that the way they start the day, the way they sustain themselves through the day, and very often the way they finish the day and the week is all built on these habits of excellence.
