# TREND_PULLBACK_DETERMINISTIC — causal scanner-plan execution (0.3.0)

This version has no agent decision loop. On the one revealed reclaim-day bar
carrying `scanner_plan`, the policy records an `ARM_BUY_STOP` using exactly
the scanner's trigger, stop, ATR, gap budget, and expiry. Every other revealed
bar receives `OBSERVE`.

The deterministic execution engine is the sole authority for fills, gap/expiry
cancellation, stops, target1/target2, breakeven after target1, and end-of-window
flattening.

## Admission (unchanged from 0.2.0)

True EMA tag (low ≤ EMA), body dip (close ≤ EMA in window), depth 4–18%,
extension ≤4%, cooldown 12 bars, SPY > SMA50, SMA50/200 uptrend.

## Construction (new in 0.3.0) — pre-registered vs 0.2.0 n30

| Piece | 0.2.0 | **0.3.0** |
|---|---|---|
| Trigger | reclaim-day **high** (chase) | reclaim-day **close** |
| Stop | pullback low − 0.15×ATR | same |
| T1 | prior swing high | **entry + 1R** (R = trigger − stop) |
| T2 | prior high + measured move | **entry + 2R** |

Hypothesis: 0.2.0 lost EV by entering extended (high) with asymmetric structure
targets that were often too close or too far. Risk-fixed 1R/2R + less-chase
trigger improves average fill quality and RR without changing who gets selected.
