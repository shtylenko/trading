Knowing how important sleep was to my trading, my sleep score from the Aura Ring became an integral input into my daily report card morning temp check. I know many of you are already familiar with it, but for those of you who aren't, without diving into it too much, I would give myself a grade A through F each morning on what I called a temperature check. This was largely based on my sleep score and it set the tone for the day. If I slept poorly the prior night, like really bad, I simply wouldn't trade that day and I would do productive work instead. If I was really well slept, I might consider increasing my risk and sizing up if it was an active day.
