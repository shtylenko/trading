As a trader, having edge is crucial to consistent profitability. Edge also has a related meaning when used in phrases like having an edge. In that case, an edge is a factor or advantage that increases the long run expectancy of your bets to above zero. There are several types of edge that a trader can exploit and they exist due to various market dynamics and inefficiencies.
