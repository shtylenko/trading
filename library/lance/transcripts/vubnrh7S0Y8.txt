Title: What are “In-Play Stocks”? #stocks #trading #daytrading
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=vubnrh7S0Y8
======================================================================

Now, index funds and ETFs dominate inflows. What this means is that the overwhelming majority of stocks are mostly just moving with the index most of the time. That means it has become even more important for traders to find in-play stocks, or what I like to call finding the broken slot machine. These are stocks that are making large, high-volume moves that are idiosyncratic from the market.
