Title: Do This Before You Start Trading with Real Money #trading #papertrading #tradingmistakes
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=2aphcMjhp8A
======================================================================

The third sin is risking real capital before you've proven you have an edge. I've watched countless traders jump straight into real money because they want the emotional rush or the real experience. All that does is accelerate unnecessary losses. You earn the right to trade real capital by proving with data that something actually works. Until then, your job is to learn, not to make money. My email is littered with countless horror stories of traders that started risking huge amounts of capital before they had edge. These are people that have lost college funds and nest eggs. I understand the human desire to jump right in, but if you are one of those people, trust me, you do not want to lose a huge chunk of that money before you even know what you're doing. Once it's gone, it's too late. There is no undo button in this business. Instead, what you should be doing is starting off in a demo account where you aren't risking real money. You want to collect data on all your trades to see if they perform in a demo environment.
