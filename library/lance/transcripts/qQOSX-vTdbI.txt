Title: How to Recover from Loss #trading #tradingmistakes #tradingstrategies
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=qQOSX-vTdbI
======================================================================

A lot of people fall for the trap of hopelessness where they wrongfully think to themselves, "If I reduce my size to X, it'll take me 200 or 300 or 500 days to get out of this hole." But here's the thing. When you start to cut your size or cut your limits, that doesn't mean you're going to be on those reduced limits forever. That doesn't mean you need to make it all back in the same one trade that you lost it in. Again, this is a long-term process to get you back on the road gradually and safely, right? So resist that urge to think, "Oh, it's going to take me x amount of days." Whatever it is, it is.
