This is the seven-step annual game plan that some of the world's top trading firms pay me to teach their traders and the same process I use to make over a hundred million dollars in verified trading profits. Step one, do your annual review because your game plan should be built based on your actual results and driven by data. Step two is to ask the most important question. What are the biggest constraints to my trading right now where if removed I could double or triple my trading P&L in 2026. Once you've identified your major constraints, we're then on to step three which is ranking them in priority. Now the critical step four, rank these constraints and focus on only one at a time. Step five, dissect how to solve the constraint and break it down into daily actions. Step six, and this is always overlooked, optimize your environment as much as possible. Now for step seven, do not be afraid to adapt and iterate.
