The sixth sin is not listening to the chart. Traders love opinions, narratives, and predictions. The problem is the market doesn't care what you think. The chart is the final vote. I've seen traders argue with price and justify why something should go up and ignore clear signals that they're wrong. The chart tells you when you're early, when you're wrong, and when the trade is dead. Your job is to listen, not to debate it. If you want to succeed, no ego, amigo.
