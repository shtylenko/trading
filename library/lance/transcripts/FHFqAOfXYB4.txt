Title: This Trading Mindset Will Decide Your Career #tradingmindset #tradingpsychology
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=FHFqAOfXYB4
======================================================================

The truth is that markets, most of the time, are quite boring. Especially at the start of my career, things were really slow. We could go weeks without a solid or big trade. If you sat on your butt that whole time, you were not going to get anywhere fast. It required us to make the most of learning from past opportunities, past charts, and past situations so that we could best apply them the next time they happened. I wanted to be able to capture the trade the first time I saw it, not the third, fifth, or even the 10th, like most other traders do. This is why I firmly believe that a trader who works with urgency and intention can make five times, even 10 times the progress of someone who doesn't. That gap compounds brutally over time. Over a year, it is incredible. Over five years, it might even be career defining. And over 10 years, it's the difference between someone who figured it out and someone who's still just working on it or having given up. This concept is especially important for the part-time trader that does not have the same amount of hours to dedicate to their craft. If you were trying to pursue this full-time, it becomes even more important that you maximize your productivity. That means you need to be doing your daily report card. You need to be doing your chart reviews. And you need to be refining your playbook, even if you only have a couple of hours that you can steadily devote to it.
