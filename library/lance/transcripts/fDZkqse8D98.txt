Title: What would 200 days of getting 1% better do for you trading? Lance’s “post-it countdown.” #trading
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=fDZkqse8D98
======================================================================

This quick trick that I called the posted countdown was always my number one priority goal each year. I focused my time and energy on this more than any outcome goals like ranking within the firm or yearly P&L. That goal was simply 200 days of getting 1% better. I would track the days I got 1% better by counting them down and keeping a tally on a post-it note. so many days, especially on the negative P&L ones. This goal pushed me to focus on making a 1% improvement rather than leaving for the day to wallow in my own sorrows. Why was this so simple yet so effective? It created a tangible countdown right on my desk in front of me that I got to physically mark off after each day. It stares at you all day and you get to see the progress you make towards the end goal with each day you check off.
