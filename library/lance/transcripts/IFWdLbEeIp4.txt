Hotkeys help you execute your edge with less friction, less hesitation, and fewer errors. Hotkeys reduce the time between decision and action. They standardize your execution, and in fast-moving stocks, like those moving on fresh breaking news, execution might be the difference between cutting your losses quickly or blowing up. The beauty of perfecting this part of your trading is that it is often a one-time investment of effort that will pay dividends for the rest of your career in every single trade that you make. At the simplest level, hotkeys are keyboard shortcuts that trigger an action. Most of us are familiar with control C and control V for copy-paste. Yet, we haven't taken the time to create similar hotkeys for our trading. These hotkeys can help us more efficiently and effectively execute common trading actions: sending orders, canceling orders, modifying order routing, changing share size, setting stops, and more. You can do just about anything with a hotkey.
