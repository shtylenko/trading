Title: How to Know When You’re Trade Position is Too Big #trading #tradinglessons #tradingpsychology
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=je426iF_lCE
======================================================================

What I learned early on is you never want to size a trade so big that it'll negatively impact how you trade the position. That was lesson three. You know you size the position too big if it results in you taking an improper stop, not holding until the correct exit rule, or unnecessary churning of the position.
