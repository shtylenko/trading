Check out this intraday chart of GME on January 28th, 2021. Absolute meltdown with mind-blowing volatility. Where this gets scary is if you're buying and averaging down as it goes lower. If you buy $210 thinking this is overextended, guess what? We just went another 100 points. I can only imagine how many people are getting liquidated in this move and getting blown up. So, the key is to wait for the counter trend. I would define that here as the break of prior bar highs. So, I would be buying in this bar if possible and trying to catch as much of this move higher as possible. When I started to wait for the counter trend, I stopped drawing down significantly. That is one of the first things that transformed my big losses into big gainers.
