Chat GPT can deeply analyze your data every single day. You can upload or paste your report cards and trading stats, win rate, average reward, average risk, expectancy per setup, and ask it to look for patterns. Here's a prompt I use. Analyze these trading report cards and trading stats. Identify what changes could best improve my trading and what patterns I might be missing. It might spot that your short setups have half the edge of your longs or that your P&L curve tanks every Friday or that you trade worse after two green days. I've told this story before, but I'll tell it again. I had one trainee that thought he was amazing at IPOs. So, he would trade every single IPO. What he didn't realize is that he was making money in the hot IPOs, but then giving half that P&L back in the mediocre ones. Having not deeply analyzed his data, his sole conclusion was that he was a positive expected value IPO trader. He didn't notice that blind spot until I pointed it out. Imagine how many other blind spots we have, but AI is going to help us find. You could feed it text-based summaries or data files. You don't even need screenshots. Just describe your numbers clearly.
