Title: One of the biggest misconceptions about trading 🤨
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=ux1yG1rx96c
======================================================================

One of the biggest misconceptions about trading is that successful traders follow some clean linear path to profitability. That's almost never reality and that's normal. This job is brutally hard. Trading is not a get-rich-quick scheme. It's a potentially get-rich-slowly after years of hard work, setbacks, and perseverance profession. And even once you start succeeding, the struggle doesn't magically disappear. There will still be rough patches of doubt, drawdowns, and periods where you feel like you're regressing. What many traders don't realize is that progress in trading is often non-linear. You can be moving two steps forward and one step back for years and still be on the right trajectory. Along the way, many people will quit. You'll see a graveyard of traders left behind because this profession simply is not right for most people. But if you genuinely love the process, if you can stay obsessed with improving, if you can survive long enough to develop real edge, then eventually the rewards can become extraordinary.
