Title: Beginner trading guide in 60 seconds 📈
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=o7A3DKUC9WY
======================================================================

If you're trying to get into trading, but you don't know where to start, watch this video. Step one, don't put real money at risk. Instead, open a free charting platform and spend time changing time frames, adding volume, and watching how a few stocks move at the open. Step two, practice only one easy money setup to specialize in. Pick out one simple pattern, write down the steps of when you'll enter, where your stop will be, and when you'll take profit. The key is making sure you stick to these rules. Step three, create a repeatable process. Go back to your charts and find at least 20 examples of your pattern. Study how the candlesticks printed. Did the pattern play out differently each time? Were targets reached? There are important insights that help build your confidence. Step four, start with the smallest size your broker allows and only trade your pattern. Log at least 30 trades and write down how well you stuck to your rules. Step five, build a daily habit to become a consistent trader. Before the open, make sure you're prepared before you log onto the charts. Stick to your model and your plan during trading hours. After market close, journal your trades and write down what worked, what didn't, and what you can improve. Follow for more videos like this to speed up your trading education.
