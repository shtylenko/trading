The eighth sin is fighting the trend. This one is simple, but deadly. New traders love calling tops and bottoms because it makes you feel smart. In reality, it's ego. Trends exist because money is flowing in one direction. Fighting that flow without a clear tested reason is one of the fastest ways to blow your account. Some of the easiest money in trading comes from aligning yourself with the dominant trend and waiting for clear entries instead of trying to be a hero. Every with the trend or after a capitulation, being with the counter trend. Although the cliche is always stated, that does not mean it is not important. Our job as traders is to live with the trend. The trend truly is your friend.
