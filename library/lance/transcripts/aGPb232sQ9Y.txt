Title: Don’t Fall for this trading trap #trading #tradingpsychology #stocks
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=aGPb232sQ9Y
======================================================================

Oftentimes traders think they suddenly became incredible traders during a hot streak when in reality the market opportunity was simply phenomenal. Every morning after a big win or hot streak, you need to ask yourself, is today's trading environment actually as good as the previous days or am I emotionally anchoring to my recent success? Opportunities in markets tend to cluster then taper off. If you've ever had a really great day or a really good week or a really good month, the longer that continues, probabilistically it is actually more likely that the next day will be less good than the prior days.
