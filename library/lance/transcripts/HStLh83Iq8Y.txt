URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=HStLh83Iq8Y

Trading is a terrible career. And I truly mean that. I know that sounds strange coming from someone who's made eight figures per year trading, but hear me out because I've lived every part of this job. The good, the bad, and the brutal. And if you're thinking about becoming a trader, here are a few things you deserve to know. Most traders fail full stop. Some studies show as many as 95% of traders end up losing. Even amongst those who break even, very few outperform after you factor in fees, slippage, and the opportunity cost of all the time they spent chasing this game. The market is a zero sum game after costs. For every winner, there's a loser. And the people you're up against, institutions with edge, infrastructure, inside flow, and decades of refinement. You think you're going to outclick Citadel on your laptop with delayed quotes and a few YouTube videos under your belt? Good luck.
