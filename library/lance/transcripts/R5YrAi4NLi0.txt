Title: One of the most dangerous things about the online trading industry ⚠️
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=R5YrAi4NLi0
======================================================================

One of the most dangerous things about the online trading industry is that it creates a completely distorted perception of what real trading actually looks like. Because if you went solely based on social media, you would think every trader wins every day. Every funded trader is printing money and every Discord is full of students banking. That is not reality. Real professional traders struggle. Every legit pro trader I know has drawdowns, bad months, and stretches where nothing works. Most are nowhere near winning every day. That's normal. So, when you see traders only posting wins, only showing green P&L, and selling a lifestyle fantasy, claiming all their followers are crushing it, you should immediately be skeptical. Real trading does not look like a non-stop highlight reel. The incentives online are broken. Many influencers make more money selling the dream of trading than actually trading. And the more unrealistic the image, the better it converts emotionally vulnerable people chasing freedom and financial escape. Ironically, if you're able to consistently support yourself or your family through trading, even imperfectly, you are probably far more skilled than you realize. Because surviving in this game long-term already places you in an incredibly small minority. That's the part social media never tells you. Follow me for more trading lessons.
