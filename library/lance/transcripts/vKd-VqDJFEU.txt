Title: How to build a trading playbook 📖
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=vKd-VqDJFEU
======================================================================

You'll be a better trader than most of your competition if you have a playbook. Here's exactly how to build one from scratch if you don't know where to start. Number one, pick your best setup. Find one trade pattern to practice like a clean pullback in an uptrend or a panic sell-off bounce. Write down exactly what it looks like so you can spot it again. Number two, screenshot every example. Every time you see your setup show up, screenshot it and save it in a folder. Do this until you have at least 10 to 20 examples at the minimum. Number three, get clear on your trade conditions. Go through your screenshots and label where you'd enter, where you'd stop out, and where you'd take profit. Number four, grade each trade. Give yourself a letter grade from A+ to F based on how clean the setup was. You only want to size up on A+ setups where everything lines up. Number five, test before you bet. Paper trade your setup for at least 20 trades to prove it actually makes money over the long run and that you can identify the pattern in real time. Do not risk real cash until you confirm your edge.
