I either get so hyped or totally scared whenever there's a big move. There's no [music] in between. Well, yes, there is. I have a playbook for volatility spikes and it helps me make decisions based on proven setups. Well, I always size up whenever there's a big opportunity. Always. I only size up if the opportunity fits my criteria for A, B, and C trades and daily loss caps. Whenever I have a bad luck trade, I'll try to win it all back ASAP. That's not me. I'll review whether it was a good loss or a bad loss. I then journal it and move on to the next trade once I've learned from it. Well, I'm always stressed when I get big losses to the point where I'd smash anything in sight, including my monitors. I see losses and volatility and strategies as part of the learning process. I know that even great strategies have rough patches. I never review my bad periods because why dwell on the past? I'd rather focus on finding a new holy grail. Well, then you will never grow, my friend. I always run targeted reviews on each trade and use my daily report card to make my system more reliable and better. All right. All right. I'm sold.
