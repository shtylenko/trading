Title: How do traders mislead themselves? 😓
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=TS_g9_ihzdw
======================================================================

How do traders mislead themselves? By letting their average price dictate how they manage the position. This is one of the most common losing behaviors in trading. The market does not care where you entered. Your average price has zero bearing on the expected value of the trade at any given moment in time. The only thing that matters is this. If you had no position on right now, would you still want this trade here? Great traders manage positions based on current opportunity and probabilities. Bad traders manage positions based on an emotional attachment to their cost basis. That distinction changes everything. Follow me for more trading lessons.
