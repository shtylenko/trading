Stage five, the advanced trader. The advanced stage is where we really start to push our size and make final refinements to our strategies, particularly adapting to the main tail events we've now seen over our career. This stage is where I recommend a trader start to pay more and more attention to trading psychology and where I even think you can justify the cost of hiring a trading psychologist to truly optimize your mental state to boost performance. We always want to be open-minded to growth, but truly mastering your niche can lead to exponential profits. Remember that cutting out your consistent losers and sticking to your core playbook pays off the most. Whatever your dreams are as a trader, odds are it can be achieved once you're flirting in this territory.
