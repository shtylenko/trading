Title: When Can Traders Start to Upgrade Their Lifestyle #trading #tradingpsychology #riskmanagement
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=hlQOFOXx7a8
======================================================================

Let's say you do make it and become successful, and you start to compound your capital base at some fantastic rates. If so, you'll be thankful for every extra dollar you saved that is now in your trading account. The power of compounding is incredible. So, if you got that used car rather than the new one and saved 15,000, you saved another 20,000 on rent by not upgrading apartments. Boy, will that money add up big over time if you're compounding at 25% per year. Now, look, you do not need to live conservatively forever. As you get more and more experience and sustain more and more market cycles, your career risk actually goes down. You've proven your edge and your ability to adapt. As that happens, the risk units from your career might go from an eight down to a six, and then down to a four. That is when you can then proportionally start to scale up your spending a little bit. With years of track record under your belt, it's okay to live in a nicer apartment or get a new car. But, always, always, always live aggressively below your means.
