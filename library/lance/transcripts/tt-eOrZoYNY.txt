Most trading careers are short-lived. Just like athletes, we burn out. We lose our edge. The game changes. Most traders are only in the game for maybe 10 to 15 years before they move on. And if you've been trading for 20 plus years, you're an absolute dinosaur in this world. That's why I always say, make hay while the sun is shining because this game will reward you like nothing else if you can survive it. But it doesn't wait for anyone and it doesn't care how long you've been around. So, how much do traders make? The real answer, most make nothing. The select few can make hundreds of millions.
