You know what every blown up trader has in common? They all had just one bad day. I get emails constantly. Lance, I had a great run and then one emotional day wiped it all out. It's not usually a bunch of paper cuts that ruin a trader. It's usually a slippery slope of poor decision-making that ultimately leads to one big colossal mistake. I know stories of traders losing millions all at once or losing 100% of their account after years of building. Sadly, there is no undo button in this game. Trust me, I've been right there with you before. If an undo button existed, I'd have mashed that button a million times by now. I [snorts] was fortunate to start my career at Trillium, a top trading firm. From day one, you have a set daily loss limit. Mine started at minus $150 per day. That ingrained in me the understanding that once that amount is lost, there's no more trading, there's no more making it back, and there's no getting back involved no matter how amazing an opportunity there is.
