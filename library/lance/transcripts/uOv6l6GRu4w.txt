URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=uOv6l6GRu4w

One of the biggest breakthroughs in my trading career was realizing this. My opinions are mostly worthless. Seriously, I can't believe the market ripped back to all-time highs. I can't believe oil is in higher. Neither really makes sense to me intellectually. [music] But here's the important part. How much money have I lost aggressively shorting the market because of my views? Zero. How much money have I lost blindly longing oil because of my macro opinion? Zero. Why? Because I don't trade my opinions. I trade charts and I trade hyper-specific setups and patterns that have shown statistical edge over hundreds and thousands of examples. And that's why so many traders go broke. They confuse [music] being right with making money. They think because they have a strong macro opinion or evaluation opinion that the market somehow owes them validation. It doesn't. The market can stay irrational far longer than your account can stay solvent. That's why price action is my North Star. If my broader view aligns with the technical setup, maybe I'll size up. But never, and I mean never, is my opinion alone enough reason for me to put risk on. Technicals first, view second. That mindset alone can save traders years of pain.
