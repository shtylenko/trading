Title: Why Selling Options Is Dangerous #options #trading #tradingstrategies
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=E5rWoJHSN7Q
======================================================================

Selling options is like writing home insurance. Every year that there isn't a catastrophe, you get more and more confident that what you are doing is genius and can't go wrong. So, what do most traders do as a result of that comfort? They get more and more aggressive in how they sell the options, implicitly taking on more and more risk at the exact worst times. And sure enough, boom, the catastrophe finally strikes. It might be a market dislocation or some huge breaking news headline, but inevitably it happens and this is how option sellers blow up or give back all their gains they earned for weeks, months, and sometimes even years worth.
