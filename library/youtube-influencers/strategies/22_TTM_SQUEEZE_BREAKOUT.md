# TTM Squeeze Breakout Strategy
## Creator: John Carter (Simpler Trading)

### Overview
TTM Squeeze identifies low-volatility periods before explosive moves. Developed by John Carter. Uses Bollinger Bands (20,2) and Keltner Channels (20,1.5). When BB inside KC = squeeze. When BB exit = firing.

### Indicator Setup
- Bollinger Bands: 20, 2
- Keltner Channels: 20, 1.5
- **Green dots:** Squeeze on (low volatility)
- **Red dots:** Firing (volatility expanding)
- **Histogram:** Momentum direction

### Entry Rules (Long)
1. Green dots for several bars (squeeze)
2. Turns red (firing)
3. Histogram crosses above zero (bullish)
4. Price above the squeeze high
5. Volume confirms (above average)
6. Enter at close or next open

### Stop Loss
- Below the squeeze low (recent swing low)
- Typically 3-8%

### Targets
- First: Prior resistance
- Use trailing stop after up 2:1

### TTM Wave
- Blue > Red > Green = strong uptrend
- Crossing signals = trend change

### Timeframe
- Daily: Best for swing trades (3-10 day holds)
- 60-min: 1-3 day swings

### Adapting for $25-100k
- QQQ daily chart + TTM Squeeze = great combo
- Individual stocks with tight squeeze
