Here is how to actually learn trading, and this is coming from an eight-figure P&L per year trader. This is how my approach went from constantly guessing and forcing trades to following a clear process over time. First, if you're a beginner, stop trading real money and focus on learning using a demo account. What you want to then do is study a small number of setups instead of chasing everything that moves. Next, define your risk before your entry. So, every trade has a clear invalidation point. After that, journal every trade to see patterns and instead of relying on memory. Finally, review weekly and make one small adjustment at a time. If you're still blowing accounts, then follow me and I'll show you the trading tips I use to live off my earnings.
