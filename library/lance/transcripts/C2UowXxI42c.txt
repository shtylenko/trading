Title: The Best Traders Learn from Their Losses #trading #tradingpsychology #tradinglessons
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=C2UowXxI42c
======================================================================

2012 was the year I learned the need to trust in the process, not the outcome. And that was lesson two. Some months I was negative almost every single day. I won't lie, that was immensely frustrating. But I recognized that as traders, losing days are inevitable. Imagine being a rookie trader and every single losing day, you leave early or you walk out in frustration rather than learning from those lessons. When you're a rookie, you will be losing most days. So automatically you are throwing away over 50% of the trading days that you can learn from. Even worse, the losing days are some of the most important days to grow. It's so important to internalize that whenever we feel frustration, it is a signal to us that there is something important we need to review and study so that we can grow as traders and humans. No doubt, it is a process to reprogram yourself to think this way. But you 100% can teach yourself to view bad days as sources of growth and be appreciative of them even though they still hurt.
