The worst thing is when you fall for that mental quicksand. And like you were saying before, so many traders will start to project. What if what if I've lost my skill? What if the market slows down? What if X, Y, and Z happens? The worst thing you can do, if you speak to any psychologist, they for any sort of issue, so often they will say, "Be careful of projecting into the future." Because when we're mentally and emotionally vulnerable, we tend to do that. And ultimately, most of our fears don't come to be realized, and it's also just not actionable. We don't know what the future's going to look like. And so often, you want to stop the projecting, stop the ruminating, and just say, "What can I control?" And go back to the process.
