Title: How to enter a breaking news trade 📈
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=SiOnoG4nKNI
======================================================================

Here's how I enter a breaking news trade as a trader with over 15 years of experience. Step one, identify the catalyst. Find the reason the fresh market moving headline is affecting [music] the stock. Step two, be first or wait for your setup. If you can act before the market has processed the news, you can make some big money. Slower traders will need their setups to develop. Step three, define your risk first. Mark your stop before you even think about entering. If your [music] risk isn't clear, your emotions will control the trade. For many breaking news trades, this will be the unaffected price before the headline hit. Step four, execute with discipline. Stick to the plan. Don't trade your emotions. Size in only when the setup confirms, not when it looks exciting. Step five, review every trade. End your day by reviewing what worked and what didn't. You cannot improve what you don't track. Journaling builds consistency. Most traders guess their entries. Follow if you're ready to learn how to trade with structure and discipline.
