Title: Don’t Let Fundamentals Ruin Your Trading  #trading #tradingstrategies #fundamentalanalysis
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=UTybMGCO9PA
======================================================================

Every single trader I know, including myself, has inevitably missed some great chart setup because they were biased about the company's fundamentals. This is how fundamentals sabotage you. They stop being context and they become your bias. Price action is the most important feedback we get as traders. It's the market telling you whether your thesis is being rewarded right now. If you have a technical system, there's one rule you need to survive. The chart is always right. Not your opinion, not your valuation, not what you think should happen. In fact, I spent years writing that saying down each morning on my daily report card because always listening to the charts keeps you out of trouble.
