[music] I logged out and I shifted my focus to what I could actually learn from it. Step two, reducing risk. [music] I cut my size and lowered my daily loss limit so I wouldn't compound the damage. I set rules for when I must size down, for when I [music] need to stop trading, or even take time off so that I always know exactly what to do [music] when future drawdowns happen. Step five, I then tried my best to rebuild slowly. I traded really small size, like 10 to 20% of my normal size, >> [music] >> and used only my highest probability setups.
