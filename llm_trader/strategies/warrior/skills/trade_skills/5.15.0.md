# Warrior 5.15 — causal scanner + 3.0 checklist watch

## Hypothesis

v5.11–v5.14 under-traded (~20% participation) relative to the sealed 3.0.0 agent
baseline (~95%). Diagnostics show two separable causes:

1. **One-minute confirmation windows** and **pattern-hard gates** reject bars that
   3.0 would take under its binary entry checklist.
2. **ENTER_NEXT_OPEN** after a four-minute causal release degraded the same 20
   names that 3.0 printed positive R on.

3.0 enters a median of **1 minute** after the scanner label via `ENTER_CLOSE`,
using the first bar where every checklist box is true (new high / break hold,
green, above VWAP, clean upper-third close, volume expansion with null-rvol
tolerance, MACD not negative).

## Contract

- Keep the causal scanner-event release (+4 minutes after the left-labelled 5m
  breakout candle completes).
- From release through 11:00 ET, evaluate the **3.0 entry checklist** on every
  closed one-minute bar.
- Patterns (candle-over-candle, micro-pullback, bull-flag) are **soft score only**
  — they never hard-gate entry.
- First full-box bar queues **`ENTER_CLOSE`** with structural stop
  `min(bar low, prior low) − $0.01` and engine +1R/+2R thirds.
- Management reuses the v5 deterministic exit ladder with exit-pressure threshold
  50 (failed-break, exit score, free-trade BE, 5m runner exit, 15:55 flat).

This is a fidelity / participation correction aimed at 3.0 parity under a
deterministic runner. Historical panels remain non-promotable (non-PIT float).
