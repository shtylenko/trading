Title: Focus on this instead of your PnL #trading #tradingpsychology #stocks
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=9cGneLjLtqQ
======================================================================

Start grading yourself based on process rather than P&L. If you can truly internalize this, you reprogram your mind and all of the sudden being right or getting revenge or catching the move becomes far less important than whether you followed your process properly. When you truly internalize the process, you do not care about any of those things. You only care about taking trades per your system.
