# Concentrated High Conviction Long Strategy — Hamid Shojaee
## Savvy Trader Founder — 781% in 3.5 Years (67% CAGR)

### Overview
Hamid Shojaee built a public portfolio on Savvy Trader that compounded at **67% CAGR** since 2022. His approach: **concentrated bets on great companies at good prices.** He holds 5-10 positions with conviction, uses fundamental research, and actively manages cash.

### Core Philosophy
- "Make larger bets on high-conviction companies and hold them long-term"
- "Valuation matters" — buy at good prices, not just good companies
- **Concentration = conviction:** top position can be 15-30%
- Cash as strategic asset: holds 25-30% cash, deploys during dips

### Theme Identification
Find long-term mega-themes with 5-10 year runways:
- AI and semiconductors (MU, NVDA)
- Space (RKLB)
- Fintech (HOOD)
- Social/AI/AR (META)
- EV/Autonomy (RIVN)
- Undervalued turnaround (BMBL)

### Entry Process
1. Research the company deeply (product, financials, competitive position)
2. Listen to earnings calls, understand management
3. Wait for a good price (valuation must be reasonable)
4. Build position over time (not all at once)
5. 3-6 positions in portfolio at any time

### Portfolio Construction
- **5-10 stocks maximum**
- Top position: 15-30% of portfolio
- Cash: 15-30% for opportunities
- Bitcoin: Small allocation
- Trim winners when they exceed comfortable size
- Sell when thesis breaks or valuation becomes extreme

### Risk Management
- Single position cap: Cap positions at 15-30%
- Thesis discipline: Sell if the fundamental story changes
- Trim into strength (Hamid trimmed RKLB at all-time highs)
- Hold cash during uncertainty
- **Concentration risk:** If wrong, one bad position can hurt; size accordingly

### Adapting for $25-100k Account
- 5-8 stocks maximum at this size
- Top position: 10-15% ($2,500-15,000)
- Start with 3-4 high-convictions, add as capital grows
- Focus on $10-200/share stocks for round lots
- Use limit orders, DCA into positions over 2-4 weeks
