Use volume to see how much participation is behind a move. Use VWAP to see if you're trading with or against big money that day. Use moving averages to spot which direction the trend is really going. Use RSI to see if buying or selling has been way too strong recently. Use ATR or average trading range to figure out how wild the stock swing is so you don't get stopped out too tightly.
