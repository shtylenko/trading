Despite all the advances in technology and trading software over the last 15 years, I still use essentially the exact same chart setup. I have not added or changed a single indicator. I haven't tweaked anything. It's all been kept materially the same. So, here is the layout. At all times, I basically have four charts on my screen. In order of importance, a 2-minute intraday chart, a 3-month daily chart, a ticks chart, and a 3-year long-term daily chart.
