Your trading strategy isn't working because you're following gurus with Lambos instead of real traders who have spent decades learning to trade. A good trading strategy is based on three things. Pillar number one, a real and testable edge. Look for a specific price pattern that is replicable. Write down the exact triggers so you know for the future. Pillar [snorts] number two, strict risk and sizing rules. Set a hard dollar limit for how much you can lose each day. Your edge will help equal out the losses in the future. Pillar number three, a repeatable process you can do daily. After each trading day, review your charts by documenting them in a trade journal.
