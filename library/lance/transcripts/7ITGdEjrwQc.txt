As an intraday trader, the intraday chart is by far the most important to me. This is where almost all of my actual trading decisions are made. If I'm taking an intraday trade, I need an intraday setup. If it's more of a swing trade, the intraday may matter less while the daily chart matters more. If I had to estimate it, the intraday chart probably accounts for roughly 60% of my decision-making process on average. On this chart, I keep things very simple. Volume, Bollinger Bands, and VWAP. That's it. Personally, I think those rainbow charts with the 50 indicators are completely ludicrous. But hey, if it works for you, great.
