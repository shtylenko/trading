Title: How to Know a Stock is Bullish or Bearish #stocks #trading #level2
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=kbSwiuHQ1X0
======================================================================

Strong bullish tape action often looks like buyers repeatedly taking offers, bids refreshing higher, sellers unable to push the stock down, and price quickly reclaiming pullbacks. Bearish tape action often looks like bids collapsing, sellers aggressively hitting the bids, failed balances, and offers constantly moving lower. One of the best exercises for developing tape reading skills is watching recordings of major market moments. These could be panic sell-offs, breakouts, short squeezes, earnings reactions, or breaking news events, and narrating the price action in real time.
