Title: If you want to stop repeating trading mistakes 💭
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=fRqY2CvnVWY
======================================================================

If you can't find good stocks to trade, you need better filters. Use a scanner that tracks the patterns you're looking for. If you keep blowing up accounts, you need real risk limits. Set hard daily and weekly loss caps and stick to them. If you trade out of boredom, you need structure. Track your setups and make a trading playbook. Only trade your A+ plays. Stay patient. If emotions control your trading, you need a system. Follow predefined rules and focus on execution, not outcome. Most traders don't need more setups. They need better habits.
