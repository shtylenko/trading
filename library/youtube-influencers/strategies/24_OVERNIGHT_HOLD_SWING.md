# Overnight Hold Swing Strategy
## Used by: Ricky Gutierrez

### Overview
Buy near market close, hold overnight/multiple days. Captures positive overnight drift and gap moves. Less screen time — decisions in last hour.

### The Overnight Drift
Stocks have statistically positive overnight gaps (~0.08% average for S&P, larger for high-beta stocks). This approach captures that drift.

### Entry Time
2:30 - 3:50 PM EST (last hour). Late enough to assess the day, early enough to get good fills.

### Selection Criteria
1. **Overall uptrend** on daily chart
2. **Above VWAP** for the day (intraday bullish)
3. **Volume above average** (institutional)
4. **Catalyst or sector momentum**
5. **Positive daily candle** (or bounce from support)

### Gap Risk Management Plan (Write Before Entry)
- Gap up 5%+: Take profit at open or hold half
- Gap up 2-5%: Hold, tighten to breakeven
- Gap down 2%+: Exit at open
- Gap down 5%+: Emergency exit

### Stop Loss
- Normal: below entry day low
- Tight: 1-2% below entry
- Trail: once up 3%+, move to breakeven
- Risk: 0.5-1% of account

### Profit Targets
- T1: Next resistance (2-5%)
- T2: Measured move
- Trail with 9 EMA after day 2

### Combination Plays
- **Bull flag continuation:** Enter near close after flag day
- **Gap and go continuation:** Strong gapper from morning, hold for day 2
- **Pullback to SMA:** Stock bounced off 50 MA intraday, enters near close

### Adapting for $25-100k
- QQQ/SPY overnight holds = safer
- Individual stocks: 3-4 positions, 5-10% each
- Avoid before Fed, CPI, earnings of held stocks
