The stock market is the world's biggest casino, because there are over 5,000 tickers. Thousands of games you could sit down at. Most of the time, trading most of those tickers, the odds are stacked against you. But, here's the twist. In the market, you actually can find games where the player has the edge. These are what I call broken slot machines. And in trading, they show up in the form of in-play stocks. It isn't always easy to find them, but taking the time to do so is essential to even stand a chance at winning over the long run. Despite the thousands of tickers out there, most traders only have maybe 25 stocks on any given day that actually offer positive expected value. And out of those, often fewer than five tend to move your P&L in a real way. You must be in the right ticker. It does not matter how good your system is. It does not matter how perfect your entries are. If you're trading noise, you have zero chance of winning. Stock selection is the number one foundational skill to making money as a trader.
