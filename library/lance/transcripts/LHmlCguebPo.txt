When I was struggling in my early years, unsure where to even begin, creating my trading playbook is what helped me figure out the simple, replicable trades that made sense to me, which I was then able to build the foundation of a career off of. This book is not about chart patterns. It's not about indicators. It's about one thing, systematically documenting and evolving your best trades so you can define your setups, codify and refine them as you then size up with confidence and become the elite trader you're capable of being. Your playbook is all the setups you've studied and documented, so you know how to effectively trade them during market
