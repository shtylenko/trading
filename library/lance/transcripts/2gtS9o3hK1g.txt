Title: How the 1% use trading strategies 💡
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=2gtS9o3hK1g
======================================================================

In 60 seconds, I'll teach you more about trading strategies than you'll learn in a year. A trading playbook is your personal rulebook. It defines when to trade, how much to risk, and what setups fit your style. Without one, your results stay random. Plus, it prevents emotional trading when you see something like this. Step one, define your edge. Know exactly what gives you an advantage, momentum plays, catalysts, or key technical levels. If you can't explain your edge, you don't have one. Step two, back test everything. Track 50 to 100 or more examples of your setups in the past. You'll see what works and what doesn't work before you ever risk real money. Step three, define risk rules. Set limits on how much you can lose in a day and on a single trade. 15 years ago, I started with a $150 loss limit, but now my max daily loss limit is $3 million and my trade loss limit is $2 million. Step four, review and refine. Every week, journal results and adjust. A strategy is never done. It evolves as the market evolves. Most traders rely on luck. The top 1% rely on systems. Follow if you want to learn how to build your own strategy that wins long term.
