How did this tiny little sheet help me make over a hundred million dollars as a trader on Wall Street? Before becoming number one at my firm Trillium and compounding over a hundred million dollars in profits, I was trading without real edge, using way too much size, way too early, and over trading. But little did I know I would finally figure out how to improve my trading systematically, how to adapt when the market changed, and stop repeating the same mistakes. I followed this three-step daily report card framework. Number one, I picked one specific process issue to work on for that day. Like overtrading, sizing too aggressively, or missing key setups, and I would ignore everything else. Number two, I graded myself each day A through F based only on whether I followed that goal and not whether I was green or red. Number three, I broke the trading day into time blocks so bad habits got caught early instead of snowballing into bigger losses. Follow for more trading education.
