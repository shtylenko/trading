Dual momentum is inspired by Gary Anton's landmark book dual momentum investing and decades of empirical evidence on momentum including research popularized by AQR Capital. Dual momentum rests on a simple foundation. Two filters that define the starting point for every decision. The first, the absolute momentum filter, compares each stock's return to the risk-free rate, the 91-day Treasury bill yield. Only those that outperform make the cut. The second, the relative momentum filter, ranks the survivors by their 12-month returns and selects the top 20. What makes Castlegate approach distinct is what comes next. After leadership is identified, the model evaluates the market regime through six carefully selected indicators. Each regime filter has a specific role. The 14-day moving average slope identifies the near-term trend direction. ADX measures the strength of that trend. ATR ratio captures volatility surges that precede reversals. VIX behavior tracks fear and complacency cycle. Market breadth ensures leadership is not concentrated in a few names. Advanced decline ratio verifies daily participation. Together, these six create a regime score that signals whether conditions are supportive or risky. They read volatility, participation, and sentiment together, helping the model know when the regime is healthy and when it's not. This regime awareness gives dual momentum its edge. It participates when the environment supports risk and steps back when it doesn't. To manage the portfolio efficiently, we follow a structured rebalancing approach. Minor rebalances are performed daily. ensuring each stock maintains equal weight and small drifts are corrected in real time. This keeps the portfolio aligned and responsive to daily market movements. Major rebalances are done every Friday. This timing aligns with weekly liquidity and reduced noise, allowing the model to capture meaningful trend shifts while avoiding overtrading. To ensure capital safety, we also use a 5% stop-loss at both stock and portfolio level, keeping drawd downs under control while maintaining flexibility to recover quickly.
