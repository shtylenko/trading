Let's find out what stocks you should be trading in five steps. Step number one, search for fresh news from the last 24 hours that forces big players to reprice the stock. Look for example at earnings reports, FDA approvals, buyouts, or major contracts. Step number two, focus on stocks breaking to multi-month highs or multi-month lows with [music] strong volume and clear momentum. Step number three, compare today's move to its normal range. If it's moving more than usual and with abnormally high volume, that's when you want to pay attention. Step four, use scanners, volume alerts, and news feeds. [music] Then cross-check Reddit, StockTwits, and X to see which tickers everyone's watching. Step number five, narrow it down. Cut the noise and focus on the five to 10 tickers [music] trading on exceptionally high volume with obvious levels from prior highs and lows and ranges expanding well beyond their usual daily move.
