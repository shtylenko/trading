Your job as a trader is to build your trading playbook. So what is a playbook? The same way a quarterback has their playbook with plays they've practiced and are able to execute effectively during game time, this is the same for you as a trader. Your playbook is all the setups you've studied and documented so you know how to effectively trade them during market hours. In short, it's your edge on
