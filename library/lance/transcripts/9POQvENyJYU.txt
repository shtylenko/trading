What are the best and worst markets for trading? We're going to rank them on a tier list. Number one, stocks. Stocks go in the S tier because they offer the best blend of tons of tickers to trade, amazing price inefficiencies like IPOs and breaking news headlines, great accessibility, and amazing scalability. That's why I love them so much. Number two, futures. Futures are B tier. Sometimes they can be very in play, but most futures markets are highly efficient coupled with fierce competition. It is essential that you don't fall for the trap of trading stuff like the ES or NQ every single day. That's where I see traders fail. Number three, crypto. Crypto is A tier. You get a solid universe of cryptocurrencies to trade that move on technicals and emotions. They have decent liquidity and lower quality competition in that market. Number four, Forex. E tier by a mile. Forex is the most efficient and boring market to trade. Having worked with thousands of traders, I've barely met any successful Forex traders. Do not fall for the BS you see online.
