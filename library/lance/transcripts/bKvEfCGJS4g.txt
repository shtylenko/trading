In the book, Bella walks through the process SMB uses to coach their prop traders from zero to seven and even 8 figure P&L per year. The first step is you want to identify a setup that works for you. Maybe it's an earnings breakdown, a panic capitulation, a VWOP reclaim, or a trend day breakout. Step two is you want to capture the context of that play. What's the bigger picture? What's the catalyst? What's the sentiment? Who's trapped? Step three is to then screenshot the trades and the charts.
