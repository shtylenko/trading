# SEPA / VCP Strategy — Mark Minervini
## 2x US Investing Championship Winner

### Overview
Mark Minervini's **Specific Entry Point Analysis (SEPA)** is a complete growth-stock momentum system. He won the US Investing Championship twice (155% and 44% returns). The core is the **Volatility Contraction Pattern (VCP)**: a stock in a strong Stage 2 uptrend pauses, and each pullback gets progressively smaller as supply dries up.

### SEPA Framework
**S** — Specific Entry Point (exact price and timing)
**E** — Earnings (accelerating growth)
**P** — Price Action (clean technical setup)
**A** — Announcement/Catalyst (institutional attention)

### The 8-Point Trend Template
***ALL 8 conditions must pass:***
1. Current price > 150-day (30-week) MA
2. Current price > 200-day (40-week) MA
3. 150-day MA > 200-day MA
4. 200-day MA trending up for at least 1 month (preferably 4-5 months)
5. 50-day MA > 150-day MA AND 50-day MA > 200-day MA
6. Current price > 50-day MA
7. Price at least 30% above 52-week low AND within 25% of 52-week high
8. Relative Strength (RS) rating ≥ 70 (prefer 90+)

### VCP Pattern Recognition
Each contraction is called a "T" (T1, T2, T3...):
- **T1**: First pullback, deepest (~15-25% pullback, ~4 weeks)
- **T2**: Smaller contraction (~10-15%, ~3 weeks)  
- **T3**: Final tight contraction (~3-8%, ~1-2 weeks)
- Each T must be **20-30% smaller** than the previous one
- Duration must ALSO contract (not just magnitude)

### Volume Analysis
- **Volume dry-up:** 40-60% below 50-day average at final contraction
- **Breakout surge:** 140-150%+ above 50-day average on breakout
- Declining volume through each successive T

### Entry Rules
1. **Standard Breakout:** Enter when price closes above pivot point on expanding volume
2. **Anticipation:** Enter during final contraction (for experienced traders)
3. **Pullback Entry:** Enter on first pullback to 10/20-day MA after breakout

Entry zone: **Within 5% of pivot point** — never chase beyond this.

### Stop Loss
- **Max stop:** 7-8% below entry
- Account risk: **1.25-2.5%** per trade
- Position size = Account risk ÷ Stop distance

### Profit Taking
- **Pyramiding:** Add 50% to position after 3-5 day confirmation above pivot
- **50-day trailing stop:** Once stock is up 20%+, trail with 50-day MA
- **7 topping signals:** Climactic run, high-volume distribution, broken moving averages
- **Sell into strength:** Take partial profits at 2-3R

### Fundamental Filters
- EPS growth: **20%+** YoY (prefer 40-50%)
- Revenue growth: **15%+** YoY
- ROE: **17%+**
- RS rating: **70+** (prefer 90+)
