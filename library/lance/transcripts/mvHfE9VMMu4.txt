I'm going to show you how I used them on an intraday basis. This was Palunteer on August 20th, 2025. The stock was in freef fall and melting lower for a few days. After an initial attempt higher off the open, we continue the melt lower and begin using the moving average as resistance. We put in a strong green reversal candle. Then that break of the moving average and break of trend was my sign that the trend might now be reversing. And I used it as an entry to go big. Just like Christian discussed in his plug example, the moving average goes from being used as resistance to then being used as support and we surf it higher while we retrace the whole move. A clean clear break and close below was a great exit. Another way I'll use moving averages is as a profit target on some of my big mean reversion plays. In fact, SMCI on February 16th, 2024 was one of my best trades of the year. And part of it was due to me shorting an exhaustion gap and recognizing that the reward on a reversal could be all the way back to that moving average on the daily chart, which was a huge distance The way.
