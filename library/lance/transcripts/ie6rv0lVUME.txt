Inside information is totally legal for prediction markets and even to some degree sports betting. Although there are some restrictions on insiders in sports. There have been dozens of instances where insiders have monetized their information in the prediction markets. So you're constantly at risk of getting hosed by people with a massive informational edge versus you. Bloomberg even recently covered an article on this citing the huge bets made right before the ouster of Venezuela's Maduro. It's a big reason that fewer than 0.04% of wallets account for 70% of the all-time profits on platforms like Polymarket. So [snorts] if you're stepping into these arenas thinking it's all fair and efficient and you're not the one with the insider information, you're already behind.
