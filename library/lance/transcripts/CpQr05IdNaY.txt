Let me explain at a high level what all effective daily trader routines should seek to accomplish. Optimal daily routines for traders should put you in the best mental state to execute well during market hours. They should reserve 60 to 120 minutes for deep work on your single biggest trading constraint, and they should include a daily review process such as filling out a daily report card. That's going to help compound your skill over time, and you should also minimize friction so that the routine is repeatable under stress and fatigue. No daily routine is going to help you if it is so complex or miserable that you don't follow it.
