# Warrior 5.14 — scanner watch / pattern-triggered entry

The scanner event selects a symbol for observation; it is **not** an entry
signal. A left-labelled scanner candle is released only after its final
constituent one-minute bar closes. From that causal release through 11:00 ET,
the deterministic policy remains in a watch state.

An entry can occur on any later revealed one-minute bar only when an implemented
bullish pattern trigger is present: candle-over-candle, micro-pullback break, or
bull-flag break. The trigger must be a green bar, at or above the scanner
trigger, with 1-minute volume expansion (≥1.5×), price above VWAP, 9 EMA at or
above 20 EMA, non-negative MACD histogram, no bearish candle-pattern veto, and
the existing 70-point confluence threshold. The decision queues an order after
that completed bar; its earliest fill is the next one-minute open with the
configured adverse execution assumptions.

Exits retain the v5.13 deterministic protection and management rules. Level 2
and tape signals from the source material are intentionally not synthesized from
OHLCV data.

This is a fidelity correction, not a fitted profitability claim. Do not tune it
on historical panels; validate it through the frozen forward-shadow protocol.
