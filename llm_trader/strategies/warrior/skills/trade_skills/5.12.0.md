# Warrior 5.12 — causal scanner-event-close confirmation candidate

This sealed deterministic candidate retains the v5.11 entry, stop, bracket, and
management rules. The only behavioral change is scanner-event availability.

## Point-in-time scanner contract

Market-data bars are left-labelled. Therefore a scanner breakout labelled `09:35`
summarizes the interval `09:35` through `09:39` and cannot be acted on at `09:35`.
The replay warms indicators privately from 09:30, but publishes its first policy
tick at `09:39`: the completed scanner bar's final minute. On that tick only, the
policy receives a `scanner_event` containing the known trigger and causal prior-day
RVOL. The historical prose reason is deliberately withheld because it includes a
non-PIT float snapshot and is irrelevant to the deterministic policy.

No tick before the release minute is visible. Candlebar events and the five-minute
context use completed OHLCV bars only. Missing minutes never form a completed
five-minute bar.

## Entry

One entry attempt, before 11:00 ET, is allowed only on the scanner-event release
minute. Require the close to remain at or above the recorded trigger (and no more
than 1% below it), above VWAP, a session high, non-negative MACD, no bearish
candlebar event, and entry score at least 70. The one-minute structural stop is
the current low minus $0.01. Engine-owned scale limits take one-third at +1R and
one-third at +2R; the remainder is a runner. No adds or re-entries.

## Management and audit

Use the unchanged v5.11 deterministic exit-pressure, failed-break, five-minute
runner, break-even, and 15:55-flat rules. Persist every score/component/reason code.
Batch audit must reproduce decisions exactly from the sealed stream and policy.

This is a causality correction, not an alpha claim. It must be evaluated on a
previously unused panel and compared only after that panel is frozen.
