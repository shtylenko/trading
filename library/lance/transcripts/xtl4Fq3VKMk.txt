Risk management comes first, always. Here's how most people blow up, and here's how to not let it happen to you. Prediction markets feel safe because your max loss is defined. At the same time, you can always see the big winners on the leaderboard. So, it feels like people are making crazy sums of money, leaving you filled with envy and greed. The truth is, a select few whales on Polymarket make most of the money. A lot of those whales are operating on inside information, and the majority of bettors just get chopped up paying spreads all day. The same way you manage your risk and emotions in trading, you should still set risk limits in prediction markets because the truth is that you always need guardrails. No matter how confident you get, you can always be wrong. Someone can always have insider information, or there can be some nuance you missed in a detail of the contract.
