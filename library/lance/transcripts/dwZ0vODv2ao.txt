Let's dive into a few examples to give you a better idea. This first stock is Circle, and this stock was in play for weeks. The stock priced its IPO at $31 per share, opened over a 100% higher, and then went on to squeeze as high as almost $300 per share. There was a ton of price discovery, volatility, and volume on this stock. It traded cleanly and made some massive moves. This ticker was one of the most opportunistic tickers of 2025. Another ticker is this ion. This is a quantum computing name which has been an incredibly hot sector. This stock has made some beautiful legs higher and over the summer we had a multi-month consolidation before breaking out of the range with the news catalyst. That technical pattern and breakout led to this stock being in play for weeks. Finally, we have Qualcomm on October 27th, 2025. This stock had huge news given that it announced its aggressive entry into the artificial intelligence data center chip market with the unveiling of two new AI accelerator chips. The news caused Qualcomm to spike as high as 20% during the day. That news catalyst caused the ticker to be in play.
