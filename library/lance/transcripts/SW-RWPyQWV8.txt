URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=SW-RWPyQWV8
======================================================================

Imagine if a breakout could make you $8 75% of the time, but you will lose $14 25% of the time. Should we be scared of this trade because our losses are in absolute terms bigger than our winners? No. If we run the math, this trade has positive expected value of $2.50, which generally means we'd want to take that trade every time. It's a great trade.
