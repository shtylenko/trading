# Episodic Pivot (EP) Trading Strategy
## Creator: Pradeep Bonde (StockBee) | Popularized by: Kristjan Qullamaggie

### Overview
The Episodic Pivot (EP) is a catalyst-based momentum strategy. When unexpected good news hits a previously neglected stock, it triggers a multi-day or multi-week repricing. Kristjan Qullamaggie turned $5K to $100M+ primarily using this strategy. Pradeep Bonde developed the MAGNA53 framework.

### Core Concept
Unexpected news + neglected stock = explosive multi-day/multi-week move. Institutions must scramble to build positions, creating sustained buying pressure.

### MAGNA53 Framework
**M** — Massive Acceleration: Triple-digit earnings growth or dramatic profit improvement
**A** — Acceleration in Sales: Sales growth >39% for two consecutive quarters
**G** — Gap Up: Stock gaps 10%+ on the catalyst announcement
**N** — Neglect: Stock has been under the radar for months/years (no major run-up)
**A** — Analyst Upgrades: At least 3 analysts raise price targets post-catalyst
**5** — Short interest of 5+ days to cover (creates squeeze potential)
**3** — 3+ analyst upgrades validate institutional recognition

Additional: CAP10x10 — Market Cap < $10B and IPO < 10 years old

### Volume Requirements
- **2x** average daily volume (absolute minimum)
- **3-5x** preferred for highest-probability setups
- Volume concentrated in **first 30 minutes** of trading
- Gaps on light volume (<2x) fail 70-80% of the time

### Entry Methods

**1. Opening Print Gap (OPG) Entry**
- Enter at market open at whatever price is available
- Set immediate stop (2.5% standard, 10% for high-conviction)
- Simplest method, catches the full move

**2. Opening Range Breakout (ORB) Entry**
- Wait for first 5-30 minutes to establish opening range
- Mark the high and low of the range
- Enter when price breaks above range high on volume
- Stop below range low (typically 5-8%)

**3. Delayed Reaction Entry (DRE)**
- Wait 2-5 days for stock to consolidate near highs
- Enter when price breaks out of consolidation on volume
- Stop below consolidation low

### Stop Loss Placement
- **Standard:** 2.5% below entry
- **High conviction:** 10% stop (larger position, less certainty on entry)
- **ORB:** Below opening range low
- **DRE:** Below consolidation low
- **CRITICAL:** Never widen stops after entry

### Profit Taking
**Pradeep Bonde's approach:**
- Hold 3-10 days
- Take 1/3 profits at 20%
- Take 1/3 profits at 40%
- Trail remainder with 10 or 20 EMA

**Qullamaggie's approach:**
- Hold 3-7 days average
- Sell 1/3 to 1/2 on first 3-5 day burst
- Move remaining stop to break-even
- Trail with 10-day or 20-day MA

### Market Filter
**Qullamaggie's rule:** Only take long EP setups when the 10-day MA of the index is ABOVE the 20-day MA. When 10 crosses below 20, step aside. (Ignoring this caused his 2022 drawdown.)

### Stock Selection Criteria
1. Gap up 10%+ in pre-market or after-hours
2. Real catalyst (earnings beat, FDA approval, major contract)
3. Strong revenue/earnings growth (mid/high double-digit or triple-digit %)
4. Stock has NOT had a big multi-month run-up (neglect condition)
5. Volume > 2x average on gap day

### Risk Management
- Per trade risk: 0.25-1% (standard 0.5%)
- Max position overnight: 30%
- Most positions: 10-20% of account
- Win rate: 60-70% in good markets, 25-30% overall
- Average winner: 20-100%+
- Average loser: 2.5-10%

### Scanner Setup
**Pre-market gap scan (TradingView):**
- Gap up > 10% in pre-market
- Pre-market volume > 500K
- Market cap < $10B
- Price > $5

### Adapting for $25-100k Account
- EPs are rarer (10-20 per year) — be patient
- Use full position size on highest-conviction EPs
- Keep stop tight (2.5%) to limit downside
- Use limit orders for better fills on gap days
- Accept that many will be small positions on lower-conviction setups

### Common Mistakes
1. Buying weak gaps (small gap, low volume, no real catalyst)
2. Ignoring volume confirmation
3. Holding when stock closes below trailing MA
4. Not verifying the "neglected stock" condition
5. Trading EPs in bear market (market filter red)
6. Widening stops after entry
