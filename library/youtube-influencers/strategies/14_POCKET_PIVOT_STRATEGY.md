# Pocket Pivot Strategy
## Created by: Mark Minervini | Popularized by: Gaurav (Market Trend)

### Overview
A Pocket Pivot is an early institutional accumulation signal that lets you enter BEFORE the breakout. A volume spike within a base with small price change and close in upper half of range signals quiet institutional buying.

### What IS a True Pocket Pivot? (ALL must be true)
1. Volume > previous day AND > 50-day average volume
2. Close in upper 50% of the day's range
3. Price gain < 2-3% (small, quiet — NOT a breakout day)
4. Must be **within a base**, not at 52-week highs
5. Multiple pocket pivots = stronger accumulation signal

### How It's Different from a Breakout
- **Pocket Pivot:** Quiet accumulation within the base. Volume spikes but price barely moves. Institutions buying gradually.
- **Breakout:** Loud breakout above resistance. Volume spikes AND price surges. Everyone jumping in.

### VCP + Pocket Pivot = Highest Probability
When a stock shows:
1. VCP pattern (volatility contracting, T1 > T2 > T3)
2. Multiple pocket pivots on the RIGHT side of the base
3. Volume drying up in final contraction
4. Then a pocket pivot on the final T = STRONG buy signal

### Entry Methods

**Method 1: Early Entry (during base)**
- Buy on the close of a pocket pivot day
- Stop: below pocket pivot candle low OR below base low
- Target: base resistance (10-20%)

**Method 2: Breakout Entry (safer)**
- Wait for pocket pivots to confirm accumulation
- Enter on pivot point breakout above base resistance
- Stop: below pivot point / base resistance
- Target: measured move (20-50%)

### Stop Loss
- **Tight:** Below pocket pivot candle low (2-5%)
- **Normal:** Below base low (5-10%)
- **Never wider than 10%**

### Targets
- First: Base resistance (10-20%)
- Second: Measured move (20-50%)
- Gaurav's example: 41% in 10-12 days

### Scanner Setup (TradingView/Finviz)
```

Price change < 3%
Close > (High + Low) / 2
50-day MA rising
200-day MA rising
Price within 15% of 52-week high
Market cap > $500M
Volume > 500K
```

### Risk Management
- Risk 1% per trade
- Wait for 2+ pocket pivots before entering
- Exit if price closes below the last pocket pivot low
- Scale out: 50% at first target, trail remainder
