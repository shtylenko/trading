When multiple time frames agree, when the daily chart is saying this is the level and the intraday is giving you the same trigger, you're stacking the variables in your favor. Here's the thing. Every time frame has its own group of market participants. Scalpers are watching the 1 minute or 2 minute. Intraday momentum traders are watching the five and 15 minute. Swing traders are dialed into the daily. Institutional money, they're watching the big picture on the daily chart. So, when you're taking a trade that's only valid on the one minute chart, you've maybe got the scalpers with you. That's it. But when that setup is happening at a major level from the daily chart, now you've got scalpers watching the quick move, intraday traders recognizing the same inflection, swing traders stepping in at the daily level, and maybe even bigger money starting to build a position. You just went from a solo skirmish to fighting with an army behind
