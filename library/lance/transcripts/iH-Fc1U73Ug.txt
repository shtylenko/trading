Title: Who will get better results? ⚖️
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=iH-Fc1U73Ug
======================================================================

Two people start trading. Both started with the same amount of money, the same tools, and the same dream. But their results could not be more different. Trader A wakes up late, scrolls Twitter for stock tips, and buys whatever is trending that day. Trader A doubles down when they're losing, hoping to make it back. No journal, no plan. Trader B starts small, paper trades for a month, and journals every win and loss. Trader B tracks patterns, studies psychology, and only trades setups that they've back tested. Instead of chasing profits, trader B focuses on consistency. They grow slow but steady. After one year, trader A's account is nearly empty. They blame the market. Trader B, they've doubled their account and built the skill set they can rely on. Most people think trading is about finding the hottest stock. It's not. It is about process, discipline, and data. Most people trade like trader A and wonder why they fail. Follow if you want to trade like trader B with discipline and potential results.
