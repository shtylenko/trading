Title: Use this Trading Risk Management Strategy #trading #riskmanagement #tradingpsychology
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=tCqlIbnxhkI
======================================================================

I want you to imagine that you have a barbell on a scale. And if the weight of the barbell goes above 10, the scale is at risk of catastrophe. It's going to break and collapse. That scale is actually symbolic of your life. On one end of the barbell is your career risk. The other side is all of your financial decisions outside of your career. This includes your lifestyle, your spending, your investments, and your debts. Now, here's the part most people don't think about enough, especially if you are a beginner trader. Your career risk is enormously high. You have no job security, probably no salary, and no 401k. And most traders fail, especially if you don't have proper mentorship. So, you're pursuing this high-risk job knowing that the early years will be hard, but doing so for a potential lifetime of financial freedom while doing a job you love. But, the point remains, most traders are at a seven or an eight in terms of risk on that side of the barbell. Their career risk is really high. It might even be higher if you do a lot of shorting small caps, or you use a ton of leverage, or you tend to go on YOLO benders with half your trading account.
