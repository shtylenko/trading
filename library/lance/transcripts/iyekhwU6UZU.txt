Title: 3 books that will make you a better trader 📚
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=iyekhwU6UZU
======================================================================

Here are three books that will make you a better trader. The first book is Market Wizards by Jack Schwagger. This one taught me that every top trader has their own unique edge, but the one thing they all share is discipline. The second book is Technical Analysis using multiple time frames by Brian Shannon. This one completely changed how I think about trading. It's about getting as many traders on your side as possible. The more time frames aligning, the better your setup is. The third book is the playbook by Mike Bellfury, founder of SMB Capital. This book helped me systemize my trading setups. The key lesson, only allocate risk to what you've studied and have proven edge in. Most traders spend years looking for strategies when all they really need is structure, mindset, and repetition. These books will teach you that better than anything else. Every great trader is a great student first. Follow if you want to learn what the best actually study.
