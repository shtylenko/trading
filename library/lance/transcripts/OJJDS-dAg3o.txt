I realized I needed to aim my sights so much bigger. In the more advanced years of our career, we have the skill set developed. What we often need most is a reset on our limiting beliefs, and that is lesson 11. Sometimes all a trader needs to know is what's possible and to have someone that believes in them. There are so many traders out there that have been trading a ton of years and could easily double their P&L. They simply need to aim bigger and double their sizing, assuming of course they have edge and consistency. An exceptionally strong foundation is when you can start to really push and see just how great of a trader you can become. In these select instances, the comparison game can be beneficial. You need to know that there are big traders out there, that there's always a bigger fish, and that that there are levels to this game you could never imagine achieving on your own, but you do have the power to achieve these as long as you keep pushing higher safely and prudently without getting complacent. I was lucky. I had Mike Bellafiore of SMB Capital tell me that I needed to aim my sights so much bigger. He helps his traders do the same, and that [snorts] is what helps traders eventually become eight-figure P&L per year traders.
