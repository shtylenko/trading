Chat GPT can't give you real-time data, but it can simulate how delta exposure changes with different assumptions. If I am trading the stock while I also have a ton of options positions open on the name for calculating my exposure, I post a screenshot straight from my account and tell it to compute my net exposure based on the options and the equity positions I have. Then you can ask it what would be the most effective way to increase or decrease exposure based on what price action you are expecting. Thinking the stock won't go higher but isn't so likely to sell off a ton either. Chat GPT will help you structure the trade. For traders new to working with options or traders who want to expand their trading using options, imagine a prompt like this. Give me three bullish options trade ideas if oil prices rise another 10% in the next few weeks. It can also give you alternative viewpoints.
