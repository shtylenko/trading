Title: Trading stocks is basically like gambling 🎲
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=DdrUBypw7Ys
======================================================================

I've been a trader for 15 years and I'm not ashamed to admit that trading is probably the worst career you can get into. There's so much bad trading advice online. Everyone wants shortcuts, but no one talks about the years it takes to get past losing money before you're consistently profitable, if it even happens. I started at a trading firm trading next to some of the best traders in the world. I've made over a hund00 million in profits and also lost many millions along the way. As you grow and trade larger size, inevitably your losses grow with you. Trading humbled me. It's not about making money every trade. It's about surviving long enough to keep learning. Every dollar I lost bought me experience I couldn't get anywhere else. If you're new to trading, don't chase results, chase improvement. Start small. Focus on perfect execution, not perfect profits. Review every trade. Your journal is more valuable than anybody else's. Detach from outcomes. The moment you stop caring about being right, you start trading right. Trading isn't about luck. It's about learning how to survive.
