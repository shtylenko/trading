Don't be scared to make the jump if you aren't yet profitable. Most traders aren't profitable when they take the leap. Trying to find profitability while still working a different job is a tough task. I find trading is one of those jobs where once you are full-time and can be allin, you don't just learn and grow a little bit faster, you grow magnitudes faster. It's the same as that golf analogy. It's probably impossible to ever go pro if you only practice a few hours each week. The difference in progress between full-time pursuit and part-time hobby is incomparable. So don't think just because you aren't profitable now doesn't mean you won't find profitability. Nearly every trader I know pursued full-time trading before being profitable. I recognize how scary this decision is. Every big leap in life
