If I woke up with zero idea how to trade and wanted to make $1,000 profit in 7 days, this is what I would do. First of all, these expectations aren't realistic because developing these skills takes years. But I'll still play your game. Step one, learn before you trade. Spend your first two days watching structured lessons and focus on one specific trading setup. Do not jump into the market blind. Step two, trade demo money before real money. Open a simulated account and place five small trades each day, testing what you learn without fear or FOMO. Step three, don't reinvent the wheel. Stick [music] to your one simple setup for the week, studying the nuances that make some of these plays better than others. Step four, risk small, think big picture. Only risk what you'd pay for lunch, $10 to $20 per trade. It's not about making money yet. It's about not losing it. Step five, review everything and only then size up when the data supports it. At the end of each day, write what worked, what didn't, and what you learned. Let the data guide you. That's how you grow faster than 90% of traders. You don't need luck to make money trading, just structure. Follow if you're ready to start trading with confidence.
