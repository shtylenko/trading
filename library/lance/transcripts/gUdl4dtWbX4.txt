Crypto in my opinion is the second best product to trade because it's the least efficient in the ways that actually matter for traders. Number of opportunities huge. New listings, new narratives, meme cycles, squeezes, liquidations, arbitrage opportunities, and more. The quality of these opportunities can be absolutely incredible. On October 10th, 2025, due to all the leverage in the system, a sell-off gained steam and created massive flash crashes across crypto markets. This was the largest crypto liquidation of all time, and certain coins were briefly trading at discounts as high as 50% or more from their equilibrium levels. If you had capital and weren't trading on leverage, some of those opportunities are some of the highest expected value you'll ever encounter. That's the type of opportunity you will never ever see in markets like forex. Another example, Trumpcoin. Whatever your politics are, as a trading case study, it's perfect. Trump's team ends up releasing a coin that isn't initially listed in too many places, and traders that had the right accessibility and were quick on their feet were able to make millions of dollars, even tens of millions. Same for when Melania issued her coin. That is a crypto edge in one sentence. The news flow is less efficient, the participant base is more reflexive, and price can travel farther than it should because should isn't part of the equation with cryptocurrencies.
