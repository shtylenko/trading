Chat GPT is amazing for performing deep research on stocks and catalysts, especially if you have some of the stronger paid versions. Here's where Chat GPT starts saving you real hours. You can have it parse through investor presentation decks, company filings, sell-side equity research reports, and more. The best part? It can even summarize all those slide deck and financial filing footnotes that are sometimes impossible to read and interpret. For fundamental research, it's absolutely incredible. It's comprehension on complex information is already far better than my own. Plus, for some of the deep research reports, it does an amazing job of scouring the whole internet for sources I'd never be able to find. That being said, I still don't trust AI for trading decisions. And no matter what level analysis you do, you still need to manage risk and listen to the chart.
