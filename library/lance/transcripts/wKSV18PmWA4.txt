Title: My Main Critique of Warrior Trading (Ross Cameron) #trading #warriortrading #rosscameron
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=wKSV18PmWA4
======================================================================

My issue is not whether Ross places the trades or whether he knows how to trade or whether he is more transparent than the average guru. He clearly is. My issue is that once you combine a legitimate momentum trader with a giant live audience in a niche that is already reflexive, thin, and crowded, especially one that is often entering these trades pre-market when they are most easily influenced, you create a structural advantage that public broker statements do not fully explain. That advantage is not illegal. It may not be explicit manipulation. It honestly isn't even his fault that he developed such a big following. And if that has always been your product offering, should he really have to cut off the live trading? It is a judgment call that I would imagine very few people would say no to.
