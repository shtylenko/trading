That is how trading cycles always catch you. Amazing opportunities in markets do not last forever. Some of the best periods I've ever traded were incredibly short windows where emotional behavior in the market created absurd opportunities. Meme stocks in 2021, certain AI momentum phases. There are times when every breakout feels like it's guaranteed to work. During these periods, opportunities are everywhere and traders start confusing favorable market conditions with permanent personal evolutions. They think they suddenly became trading gods overnight. When in reality, the market environment simply became perfectly suited to their Markets are adaptive. As opportunities become obvious, more traders pile in, edges compress, and the patterns become overcrowded. And then, as you increase your sizing the most, bam! The exact moment trading begins feeling easiest is often when things are becoming most dangerous underneath the surface.
