Here are four things I would never do as an eight-figure trader. Number one, trade without a plan. If you don't pre-plan your entry, stop, and exit, the trade becomes emotional the second it moves against you. Number two, take setups that are not written down and part of your playbook. If a setup isn't in your playbook, you can't improve it, track it, or prove that you have edge over time. Number three, oversize to make it back. This can turn one bad trade into a mental spiral that can ruin an entire week. Number four, keep trading after your max loss is reached. When you keep trading after hitting your loss limit, you're no longer trading with guardrails. Want to live off your trading profits someday? Follow me and I'll show you exactly how I do it.
