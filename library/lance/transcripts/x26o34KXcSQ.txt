Step one, understand what quitting your nine-to-five actually means. You're not just replacing income. You're replacing structure, benefits, and stability. Trading full-time is running a performance business, goodby salary, and bi-weekly paycheck. If you're chasing freedom, make sure you understand the weight that comes with it. Look at your savings, figure out your monthly burn rate, and calculate your runway. One of the most important things I did before starting work at Trillium was I built up a massive war chest from working overtime at my pool maintenance job. It was miserable, but it was worth every bit.
