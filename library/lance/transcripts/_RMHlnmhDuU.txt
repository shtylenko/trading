URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=_RMHlnmhDuU
======================================================================

Everybody makes the same mistakes again and again. What trading or any skill really comes down to is how can you identify that mistake and build processes and reprogram your thinking to then just fix it over time. And that it's not some perfect, oh wow, like I'm overtrading, therefore good traders magically never overtrade again. Like every mistake there is, even at my level, 15 plus years of trading, I'm still making those same mistakes. But you just try and do more right things than you do wrong and minimize it the best of your ability.
