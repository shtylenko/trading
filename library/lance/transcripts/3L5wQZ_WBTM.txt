Title: Common Trading Constraints for Intermediate Traders  #trading #tradingpsychology #tradingconstraints
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=3L5wQZ_WBTM
======================================================================

Most intermediate traders tend to do well solving a few of these main constraints. The most common by far is increasing sizing, particularly in their best setups, and the other is expanding one's playbook so they have a backup plan when their playbook is not aligned with the hottest opportunities in the market. Other areas worth thinking about are how to find more of your best setups and how to further refine risk management to avoid unnecessary big losses. Losses and draw downs will always be a part of trading.
