Title: How to Beat the Mental Game of Trading #trading #tradingpsychology
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=rv9MwPXBaDg
======================================================================

In psychology, you cannot interrupt a pattern you do not consciously recognize. The moment you begin to identify your emotional state in real time, whether it's revenge, boredom, FOMO, frustration, greed, you create separation between the impulse and the action. Even if you still take the bad trade initially, building the habit of recognizing the emotion is massively important because awareness weakens that automatic behavior. And ironically, the goal at first is not perfection. The goal is simply shortening the delay between the mistake and recognizing it. Maybe today you realize you overtraded an hour later. Eventually, it becomes 10 minutes later, then during the trade, then before the trade. That progression is how real behavioral change actually can happen.
