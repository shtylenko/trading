# Right side of the V — SPEC (v0.1.0)

**Thesis (Lance):** Same price ≠ same EV. Do not buy the dump (left side). Wait for
the turn to confirm (right side), risk under the pivot low.

**Horizon:** Multi-day daily bars (same platform as other swing families).

## Mechanical rules

| Step | Rule |
|------|------|
| 1 | Selloff: local swing high → pivot low, drop 8–35% over 3–20 bars |
| 2 | Pivot is a local low (neighborhood min) |
| 3 | Reclaim within 8 bars of pivot |
| 4 | Close > SMA20 (not extended >8% above) |
| 5 | Close ≥ pivot + 35% of drop height (min retrace) |
| 6 | Close > prior day high (structure flip) |
| 7 | Stop under pivot − 0.2×ATR; T1 50% retrace; T2 full retrace to swing high |

**Not** dominance-flip / RSI knife-catch (`lab` f* killed that mold).
