Title: Why Athletes Make Great Traders #trading #daytrading #tradingpsychology
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=KNGUOwWHIYQ
======================================================================

There's a reason why trading firms love college athletes. Not only are they extremely competitive, but highle athletes have spent their whole lives receiving tough feedback from coaches and persevering through losses. They've learned to emotionally accept a loss and to learn from it the first time. One of my favorite trainees was a college football player. He wasn't some MIT brainiac, but it didn't matter. He was exceptionally good at taking instruction from me, saying, "Yes, coach," and incorporating it. The speed in which we can incorporate feedback is actually what I find to be the number one determinant of how quickly a trader progresses. Essentially, a powerful Y is how long of a runway a trader will give the job. Then coachability speaks to how fast they will progress while on that runway.
