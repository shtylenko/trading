Most traders fail within a year of starting their trading journey. But if you want to be a successful trader and get results like this, here's what you need to understand. Step one, build a playbook. Most beginners trade on vibes rather than systematically finding data and back-tested patterns that work over the long run. Step two, protect your mental state. Get enough sleep, avoid conflicts, and do a pre-market temp check like rating your stress and mood from one to 10 so your emotions don't take part in your decision-making process. Step three, focus on improvement. You want to always ignore other traders P&L and focus on your own game and improving your own process so that you don't spiral comparing yourself to others. Step four, understand what money means to you. Know your relationship with money. Is it excitement, fear, or frustration? This helps you gauge your risk-taking and lifestyle decisions so that they match your own goals. Step five, remember trading is just one part of your life. Prioritize relationships, friendships, and other hobbies so you have something or someone to hold on to during the inevitable ups and downs of trading.
