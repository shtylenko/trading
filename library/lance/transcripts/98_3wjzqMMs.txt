Trend. The direction the price is moving on your key time frames. Support and resistance. Price levels where the stock is unable to get below for support [music] or above or resistance. VWAP. It's the average price paid weighted by how much participants are buying. It's telling you whether buyers or sellers are in control right now. Volume spikes. This is when way more shares trade than usual in a short amount of time. Big volume confirms real moves. Huge spikes after long run signal the move might be done. Risk reward. How much you could lose versus how much you could win in a trade.
