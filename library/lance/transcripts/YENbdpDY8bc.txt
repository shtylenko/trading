How do you know whether you are trading out of boredom or trading when you have genuine edge? This is such a common question and so important for so many beginners. Many have heard me talk about the importance of having a trading playbook. This concept was popularized by Mike Bellfury and what it is is you write up all of your different plays with very specific details. What is the exact setup you looking for? what nuances you need to see in order for you to place that trade. If you haven't taken the time to define your playbook and these specifics, it's very hard to know or at least in retrospect define whether a trade was out of boredom or whether it actually met the criteria you set forth. So, so often taking the time to go through the exercise of making your trading playbook that defines what you're looking for is so useful because it allows you to answer that exact question so you can judge for yourself whether it was worth the trade or not.
