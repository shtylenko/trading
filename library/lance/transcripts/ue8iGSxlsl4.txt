Implementing a daily report card was the best practice that helped take me from being a top 10 trader to then take the number one spot at Trillium. So, what is a daily report card? The daily report card is a daily review process done with the goal of efficiently improving one's trading. This structured review pushes the trader to identify the most important aspect of their trading and it forces them to reflect and work consciously towards their progress each day. One foundational piece of metalarning is deliberate practice.
