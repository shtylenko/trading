## 2. Key Themes by Document

### Theme: ACD / First Candle New High

The core entry pattern is taught across 7+ multi-hour training series. The most-viewed entry training (dvetF0H3pNo, 886K views) is a dedicated candlestick reading guide. The pattern is consistent:

- **Entry:** First 5-minute candle to make a new high after a pullback
- **Stop:** Low of the pullback candle (not the entry candle)
- **Target:** Retest of day's high
- **Exit:** First red candle closing below prior green candle
- **Confirmation filters:** Volume must be above average, MACD must be positive

No contradictions across files — the pattern is taught identically across all training series with minor terminology variations ("ACD pattern," "first candle new high," "breakout from consolidation").

### Theme: VWAP Strategies

Three distinct setups found:

1. **VWAP as support** — Stock rallies, pulls back to VWAP, bounces. Entry on bounce candle.
2. **Sub-VWAP trap** — Stock breaks below VWAP briefly, triggers stops, then reverses sharply. His favorite intraday setup.
3. **VWAP as resistance** — Stock below VWAP, approaches it, fails to break through → short opportunity (rare in his long-biased trading).

### Theme: Icebreaker + Scaling

The icebreaker rule is his most important risk management innovation:

- Start each day at **25% of normal position size**
- Only scale to full size after earning the first profit milestone ($1,000 for his main account)
- If the day is cold and you never hit the milestone, you stay at 1/4 size all day
- This mechanically produces the 76-day green streak: small losses on cold days, aggressive scaling on hot days

Scaling adds in thirds: 1/3 base position, add 1/3 on confirmation, add 1/3 on follow-through. Exit in reverse (sell 1/3 at time).

His $583 → $10M+ story is documented in multiple files. The scaled growth path:
