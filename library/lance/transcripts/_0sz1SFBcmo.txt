Title: What’s the difference between a healthy trend and a fake move? 📊
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=_0sz1SFBcmo
======================================================================

What's the difference between a sustainable trend and an unsustainable spike? Most traders see green candles and assume both of these charts mean the same thing. But, one of these moves is sustainable and the other one is usually a trap. Step one, look at the slope, not just the distance traveled. Healthy trends move up in controlled waves, while spikes compress too much price movement into too little time. Step two, watch the pullbacks. Strong trends have shallow pullbacks and hold key levels, while spikes snapback violently once momentum fades. Step three, compare volume to price movement. When price rises steadily with consistent volume, participation is healthy. But, sudden spikes often come from emotional chasing. Step four, check distance from key moving averages. Trends stay close to the moving averages, but spikes move far away and are bound to retrace. Step five, ask why the move is happening. Healthy trends are built on sustained demand, like when a fresh headline changes the fundamentals for a stock. While spikes are often driven more by emotions, FOMO, or short-term imbalances. If you want to stop chasing spikes and start trading quality setups, hit that follow button.
