After 15 years, this is the ultimate truth. You can never ever ever get rid of risk. You can only do your best to manage it. Many traders I interact with want to grow their trading without losing more or capture more upside while never having a drawdown. You can't have yin without yang. Making money in trading only comes from embracing risk and uncertainty, even for the best traders in the world. Cutting size reduces blowup risk, but increases opportunity cost. Having tighter risk limits means safer trading, but potentially slower growth. There's no free lunch in this business, only intelligent tradeoffs. If you take one thing from this video, it's this. Your career will not end because you didn't make enough money. Almost every trader I see that blows up did it because they were trying to get rich quickly and took on more risk than they should have in a pursuit of faster growth. Always control your downside. Stay in the game long enough while constantly working hard to improve, and the P&L will take care of itself. That's how I've lasted 15 years in this game. That is how you can do the same.
