The best stop in the world is worthless if you don't take it. Professionals treat stops as non-negotiable. Pros do not move stops impulsively. They don't arbitrarily override their own system. Stops exist to protect the long-term expectancy of your trading. If you struggle taking stops emotionally, build rules around it. Use hard stops. Automate them. Use daily loss limits. Narrow the window for error. Size down to remove some of the emotions behind it. Then gradually work your way back up.
