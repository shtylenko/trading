You're trading me because you can't explain why this setup works. You're trading me because you can explain the setup in one sentence. You're trading me because price started moving fast. You're trading me because this matches a setup you've already studied. You're trading me because you're risking an amount that makes you anxious. You're trading me because your risk is small enough to stay calm. You're trading me without knowing where you're wrong. You're trading me because your stop was defined before entry. You're trading me to make money back after a loss. You're trading me as one independent decision in a long process.
