Back in late 2021, I took one of the biggest losses of my career. I lost over $2 million in Avis Group within the span of an hour or so. Ouch. I performed this exact routine after the loss. I wrote up the trade highlighting the fact that this was in fact a reversal trade that was in my playbook and that I had been otherwise making a ton of money using throughout the course of 2021. This was the same playbook I used for GameStop and AMC earlier in the year. By performing my write up and doing the reflection, I was able to identify that my key mistake wasn't so much an execution, but because of my sizing. Then upon even deeper reflection, I identified that my sizing was a result of how much I was anchoring to an outcome goal. Now, keep in mind, there will be times where you do everything right.
