Don't dwell over it. Don't ruminate. Just move on. Yes, this is easier said than done, but once you've learned the lesson and implemented solutions, the benefit of that is that you are giving yourself permission to mentally move on. Ruminating is no longer productive. You've done the work. So many traders beat themselves up again and again in [clears throat] trading and in life. We want to do our best to only focus on our process and what we can control. Once a loss has occurred, there is no reset button, no mullions. You paid the tuition, so make sure you learn the lesson. Then be a goldfish.
