Sleep was one of the strongest correlated variables to my P&L and nothing else came anywhere close. And don't kid yourself. If you think going into work tired or hung over is not going to affect you, you are flatout wrong. Let's take a professional athlete as an example. When they aren't locked in, going into game day because they were out the night before, well, it would just be unacceptable and unfathomable to even think about. Except the difference is a lot of athletes have a team to carry them and the maximum loss is often just an L for you. Your livelihood relies on your performance day in and day out. You need to perform. If you don't show up well-rested with your head in the game, you will never come anywhere close to your true potential. When you're underslept, your decisions are just a little slower, your willpower a little bit less, your focus slightly off. And in the right moment, the consequences can be dire. Not only that, but your ability to learn and progress as a trader is impaired when you aren't getting proper sleep. I know what you're thinking. I don't prioritize my sleep and I don't get eight hours. But I feel fine. If that is you, then trust me and hear me out on this. I've probably worked with over a dozen traders who said exactly that. But it was simply they hadn't realized that being sleepd deprived had become their new normal.
