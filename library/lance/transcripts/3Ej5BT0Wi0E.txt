Around 4:30 p.m., I'll start my daily review work from the day and complete my daily report card. This is where that feedback mechanism I mentioned is so critical. I want to learn as much as I can from each trading day. What were my mistakes, my misses, what did I do well? What key trading moments do I need to rewatch and restudy? All of this helps me course correct or take action to best capitalize the next day. Around 5:00 p.m., I go to the gym. For those of you who don't know, one of my oldest passions is weightlifting. I love seeing steady, quantitative progress and pushing my physical performance. I used to take powerlifting pretty seriously and still enjoy the sport.
