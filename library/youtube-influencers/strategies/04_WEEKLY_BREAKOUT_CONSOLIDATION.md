# Weekly Breakout Consolidation Strategy
## Creator: Financial Wisdom | 25%+ Annual Returns for a Decade

### Overview
A proven end-of-week swing trading strategy delivering 25%+ annual returns for over a decade. Uses weekly charts to identify lateral consolidation breakouts. Perfect for people with full-time jobs — only 1-2 hours on Sunday morning. Requires a custom scanner that does 90% of the work.

### Core Concept
Stocks don't move in straight lines. They:
1. **Advance** — Strong uptrend phase  
2. **Pause/Consolidate** — Sideways movement (building energy)  
3. **Resume** — Breakout to new highs

We buy Step 3: when the stock breaks out of a well-defined sideways consolidation on the weekly chart.

### Consolidation Criteria
- **Duration:** Minimum 6 weeks (longer is better)
- **Range:** Tight horizontal range (lateral, not volatile)
- **Depth:** Ideal pullback under 20%, avoid deeper than 35%
- **Volume:** Should contract during consolidation (especially on down weeks)
- **Price above 20-week MA** (≈ 100-day MA on daily chart)

### Breakout Criteria
- Price breaks AND closes above resistance on weekly chart
- Breakout candle is a **10-week closing high**
- Breakout move: **>5% and <20%** versus prior close
- **Volume higher** than prior week (more is better)
- Clear stop-loss level can be identified

### Entry Rules
- Enter at the **open of the following week** after confirmed breakout
- For daily charts: enter on break of resistance, but be cautious

### Stop-Loss Placement
Divide the consolidation box into three zones:
- **Upper third** → Too early, prone to shakeouts
- **Lower third** → Too late, higher breakdown risk
- **Middle third** → **Optimal placement**

Place stop in lower portion of the middle third.

**Rules:**
- Ideal stop distance: < 15%
- If risk exceeds 15%, reduce position size
- Never risk more than 2% of account (1-1.5% recommended)

### Exit Strategy (MACD Crossover)
When the blue MACD line closes below the red signal line:
- Mark that week's low
- If stock crosses below that low → EXIT
- No emotions, no second-guessing

### Market Filters
- Use 10-EMA and 20-EMA on index to assess broader market health
- When index trending: breakouts work better, follow-through improves
- Avoid trading when market is in sustained downtrend

### Scanner Setup
The bespoke scanner filters:
- Consolidation patterns (tight range, 6+ weeks)
- Breakouts (closed above consolidation box)
- Breakout size (2-20%)
- Market cap (> $500M)
- Scores on: Technical, Quality, Momentum

### Risk Management
- Base position on stop distance
- Risk 1-1.5% of account per trade
- Use structure-based stops (never arbitrary)
- Max 10 open positions (fewer for beginners)

### Adapting for $25-100k Account
- Scanner does the heavy lifting (Investors.com, Finviz, TradingView)
- 2-3 hours on Sunday is all you need
- Start with 3-5 positions at $2,000-5,000 each
- Focus on US stocks $500M+ market cap ($10-200/share)
- Use stop-limit orders to execute entries Monday open

### Common Mistakes
1. Trading consolidations deeper than 35% (too wide a stop)
2. Entering before the weekly close confirms breakout
3. Mixing daily and weekly chart analysis inconsistently
4. Ignoring volume confirmation on breakout
