### Same-bar fill rule (deterministic OHLC)

You decide **after** the full daily bar is revealed. An `ARM_BUY_STOP` logged on
bar `i` is live only for bars **strictly after** `i` â€” it does **not** fill on
the same bar you armed (even if that bar's high already cleared the trigger).

So:

| Situation on the bar you are logging | Action |
|---|---|

| High > handle high but weak close / thin volume | `OBSERVE` â€” do not ENTER_CLOSE |

## ENTRY checklist (PLAN phase)

While flat and not yet armed, each day run:

### 1. Strong uptrend?
- Close above SMA20 and SMA50 on the latest bar (`above_sma20/50`).
- Prefer close above SMA200 when the field is present; if `sma200` is `null`
  (insufficient history), **skip the 200 test** â€” do not block the plan solely
  for a missing SMA200.
- `sma50_rising` true (50 SMA higher than ~20 bars ago).

### 2. Healthy cup?
- Rounded multi-week trough (not a single-bar V).
- Depth roughly 12â€“35% from lip to low.
- Volume tends to lighten into the trough and pick up on the right side.

### 3. Tight handle? (SPEC-hard)
- Handle length **3â€“15 sessions** (hard fail outside that band â€” do not arm a multi-month coil).
- Handle depth â‰¤ ~40% of cup depth; light volume vs the cup; price coiled under the lip / handle high.

### 4. Room to run?
- No dense resistance immediately above the handle high (prior swing highs
  stacked within a small ATR of the breakout).

### 5. Market / sector alignment?
- Prefer not arming if the broader tape is clearly broken (if you have SPY/QQQ
  context in mind from general knowledge of the revealed session only â€” do not
  fetch external data). When unsure, require stock-only checklist 1â€“4.

**If any of 1â€“4 fails â†’ `OBSERVE` or `STAND_DOWN`.** Do not arm a weak handle.

### Plan numbers (objective)

Once 1â€“4 pass, compute:

```

```

Then apply the **breakout quality gate** (SPEC):

  AND (rvol is null OR rvol >= 1.3)     # volume confirmation when present

## MANAGE checklist (IN position)

### Never abandon a live plan

After you `ARM_BUY_STOP` or enter, you **must keep** the loop
(`step next` â†’ `resolve` â†’ log) on **every** subsequent bar until one of:

- `resolve` shows flat **and** you have no pending entry (filled then stopped/exited, or you `CANCEL_ENTRY`), or
- you log terminal `STAND_DOWN` (setup invalidated before fill; no re-entry), or
- `step next` prints `STATUS end`.

**Stopping the agent while a buy-stop is still armed is a failed run.**
Flat + live ARM is **not** done. Do not exit the loop after "plan complete, waiting."

1. **Hard stop** â€” engine stops you if bar low â‰¤ stop (`resolve` will show flat).
   Log a one-line note next bar; **do not re-enter** the same setup.
2. **T1 hit** â€” half position scaled; move stop to break-even (`SET_STOP` at
   `avg_entry`) once T1 is filled if still open. Prefer placing T1 `SCALE_LIMIT`
   on the fill bar (or next bar if you need a second action).
3. **T2 / trail** â€” let remainder run to T2; optional trail under higher lows /
   SMA20 after T1, never loosening the stop.
4. **Invalidation** â€” close below SMA50 with expanding downside volume after
   entry â†’ `EXIT_CLOSE` (thesis broken).
5. **Time** â€” if still open near end of stream (`max_hold_bars`), allow engine
   auto-flat at session end; prefer an earlier plan-based exit.

**Do not** scalp intraday noise. These are daily bars.
