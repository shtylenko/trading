One of the most important skills in trading is correctly identifying the market regime you're in. Because the strategies that make you a fortune in an uptrend are often the exact same strategies that destroy you in a downtrend. This is why having a clear rule set for determining trend is so critical. In strong uptrends, aggression gets rewarded, breakouts work, and momentum follows through. But in downtrends, those same behaviors become traps. Breakouts fail, bounces get sold, and one of the biggest edges in trading is simply recognizing what environment you're actually in and aligning your strategies accordingly. You do not need to predict every twist and turn perfectly, but you absolutely need a framework for identifying, [music] are we trending up, trending down, or chopping sideways?
