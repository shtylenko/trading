What value has my trading added to society? My trading has cushioned market panics. My trading has dampened euphoria. My trading has helped price the latest news headlines into markets so that the rest of the world can receive the most accurate prices. My trading has prevented fraudulent companies from raising more capital. My trading has helped the pension funds, retirees, and average Americans be able to reliably buy and sell securities. In the aggregate, we allow others to financially participate in the growth and success of the United States through the equity market. We don't do it alone. We do it as part of a holistic system along with hedge funds, value investors, quant funds, and more. But the same way you shouldn't downplay the role of any person at a company, you need the assistants, the secretaries, the janitors, the tech staff. Markets in the same way need day traders. Take away any one of us and markets will function. Take away day traders as a class and markets will be hobbled.
