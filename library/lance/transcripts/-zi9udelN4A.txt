The only bad loss is one you don't learn from or one that takes you out of the fight. Your tuition has already been paid. You already took the loss. You can't undo it. So, you might as well take the class and learn from it, right? Start to dissect the trade. You want to identify all the primary and secondary causes of the loss. By primary cause, it might mean maybe you didn't take your stop. Maybe you messed up an entry. Maybe you chased. Then, what I mean by secondary cause is, is there something psychological that could have led to that? Maybe you felt FOMO. Maybe you were on tilt. Maybe you were poorly slept or something. Then you want to propose thorough solutions that might help you in the future. When possible, ask other traders for their thoughts and feedback on how they might have faced or resolved this issue as
