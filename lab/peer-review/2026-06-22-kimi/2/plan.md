# Plan: Automated Stock & ETF Trading Strategies Research

## Objective
Find automated, reliable, repeatable stock & ETF trading strategies with >3% monthly profitability. Constraints: no futures, no shorting, no options — only long positions in stocks and ETFs.

## Stage 1: Deep Research (Parallel Swarm)
Load `deep-research-swarm` skill. Deploy parallel research agents across strategy categories:

- **Agent 1: Momentum & Trend Following Strategies** — Research momentum-based systematic strategies (dual momentum, relative strength, trend-following with moving averages) with documented backtests showing >3% monthly returns.
- **Agent 2: Mean Reversion & Statistical Arbitrage** — Research mean reversion strategies (Bollinger Band mean reversion, RSI oversold bounces, pairs trading with ETFs) suitable for automated long-only execution.
- **Agent 3: Factor-Based & Quantitative Strategies** — Research factor investing strategies (value, momentum, quality, low volatility) and quantitative screens with proven automated execution.
- **Agent 4: ETF Rotation & Sector Strategies** — Research ETF rotation systems (sector rotation, asset class rotation, momentum-based ETF switching) with monthly rebalancing.
- **Agent 5: Seasonality & Calendar-Based Strategies** — Research calendar effects (January effect, turn-of-month, holiday effects, Presidential cycle) and seasonal patterns in stocks/ETFs.
- **Agent 6: Risk Management & Position Sizing** — Research Kelly criterion, volatility targeting, dynamic position sizing methods that can enhance strategy returns while managing drawdowns.

## Stage 2: Validation & Cross-Verification
- Cross-validate all findings for profitability claims
- Verify backtesting periods and out-of-sample performance
- Check for survivorship bias, look-ahead bias, transaction cost assumptions
- Rank strategies by reliability, repeatability, and net profitability after costs

## Stage 3: Report Compilation
Load `report-writing` skill. Compile validated strategies into a comprehensive report with:
- Strategy descriptions
- Entry/exit rules
- Historical performance metrics
- Risk characteristics
- Implementation guidance
- Recommended broker/platform for automation

## Deliverable
Final report in .docx format with actionable, evidence-based automated trading strategies.
