Afterhours trading is similar in concept to premarket, but I find that the largest opportunities after hours tend to come from stocks that are reporting earnings. The biggest and most followed companies in the world tend to report their earnings after hours, particularly those in the tech space. Companies like Tesla, Nvidia, and Apple all report their earnings after the close, not before. And trading these earnings releases can be incredible opportunities for traders. The same disclaimers apply in that you need to be careful upon the initial release of the earnings because of the thinner liquidity and wider spreads. Trading earnings after hours should only be reserved for experienced traders due to how quick and severe these moves can be. Another great opportunity after hours for traders is in microcaps, which often use that session to release news of a secondary offering. For these companies, raising capital is not a rare event. Dilution is a tool they use regularly, and afterhours announcements allow information to be released after their stock has been run up during market hours. That is another great opportunity for breaking news traders, and also something to be aware of if you are longing some of these microcap stocks past a regular trading hours session.
