Some of the most common constraints for beginners, which if resolved can lead to outsize results, are these. First and foremost, we have stock selection. So many struggling beginner traders that reach out to me are trying to make money trading the S&P 500 every day, not realizing they're playing this game on hard mode. You need to be trading in-play stocks and building the skillset of how to effectively find these on a daily basis. If you are studying stocks with no edge or opportunity, you are just wasting your time. Effort does not matter if it's totally ineffective effort. Other issues beginner traders have is not knowing how to build a trading system to capture their plays. They do not even know where to start in finding plays with edge. They don't have an effective daily routine and they're trading alone rather than with a trading pod.
