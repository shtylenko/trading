Title: How to build a trading playbook in 2026 📘
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=JzK8N8n8jqU
======================================================================

Everyone tells you to start trading in 2026, but nobody actually shows you how to do it profitably. Step one, define your setups. Write down the exact setups you trade, including the pattern, conditions, [music] and what makes that setup valid. Step number two, set clear risk rules. Decide how much risk per trade and where your setups are proven wrong before you ever buy or sell. Step number three, collect examples. Screenshot both the good and bad trades so you can see patterns instead of relying on your memory. Step number four, grade your execution. You want to judge your trades by how well you followed your rules, not just by whether you made money. If the markets have been kicking your butt, then follow for more tips to find real edge.
