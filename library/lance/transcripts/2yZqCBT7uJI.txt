I am here to teach you my three-step recovery plan for dealing with large losses, and it just might save your career. Step one of the three-step loss recovery plan is to objectively review and write up the trade while the thought process and experience is still fresh in your mind. What's next is step two, propose solutions. Many traders will take the time to identify why the loss happened, but critically they won't do that next step of identifying solutions to prevent losses like that from happening again in the future. This is one of my biggest peeves when working with young traders. From my experience, the absolute best traders, they impress me both in the urgency with which they come up with new solutions and the thoroughess of the solutions they think of. Step three after that is to then build and implement safeguards. Proposing amazing solutions is great and all, but they're also ultimately useless if you don't actually implement them. Step three is all about taking the actions needed to ensure as high of a chance of compliance as possible.
