# Warrior 5.7 — causal scanner-event parity candidate

This is a deterministic selection candidate. No language model makes trading decisions. The batch
runner publishes the sealed causal stream, executes
`strategies/warrior/policy.py`, records one auditable intent per completed minute,
and lets the deterministic OHLC engine own fills, sizing, costs, stops, and target
resolution.

The transcript remains the source for the trading ideas. The numeric weights and
thresholds below are explicit engineering hypotheses, not probabilities and not
numbers attributed to Ross Cameron.

## Data contract

- Replay begins at 09:30 ET. Scanner trigger data is hidden until its recorded
  trigger minute, when it is released as a one-time causal `scanner_event`.
- Candlebar events are computed deterministically from completed OHLCV bars only.
- `PatternEvent.score` is geometry quality in `[0,1]`, not a forecast.
- A five-minute bar is emitted only when all five clock-aligned constituent
  minutes are present. Missing data never becomes a synthetic "complete" candle.
- A five-minute break is not eligible until `prior_3_count == 3`. Until then,
  `prior_3_high`, `prior_3_low`, `prior_3_avg_volume`, and `volume_ratio` are null.
- Only current and previously completed bars may influence a decision.

## Entry

Only one entry attempt is permitted, before 11:00 ET, and only after the one-time
scanner event has occurred and on a tick carrying
a completed five-minute candle. Every hard gate must pass:

1. a scanner event has been received and the close remains at or above its trigger;
2. `prior_3_count == 3`;
2. five-minute candle is green and closes in its upper third;
3. five-minute close is above current VWAP;
4. five-minute high and close both exceed `prior_3_high`;
5. `volume_ratio >= 1.5`;
6. current `macd_hist >= 0`;
7. no current bearish candlebar event; and
9. entry score is at least 75.

Entry score:

- Pattern, maximum 30: take only the best current bullish event across 1m and
  completed 5m. `bull_flag_break = 30 × quality`,
  `micro_pullback_break = 27 × quality`, and
  `candle_over_candle = 22 × quality`.
- Volume, maximum 25: +15 at `volume_ratio >= 1.5`, +5 more at `>= 2.0`, and
  +5 when current `rvol_bar >= 1.5`.
- Trend, maximum 20: +8 above VWAP, +6 for `ema9 >= ema20`, and +6 for
  non-negative MACD histogram.
- Candle quality, maximum 15: +10 for the required green upper-third close and
  +5 when no current bearish event exists.
- Timing, maximum 10: +10 before 10:30, +5 through 10:59. At or after 11:00,
  new entries are prohibited; existing positions continue under the same
  management rules.

The structural stop is:

`min(bar5_complete.l, bar5_complete.prior_3_low) - $0.01`

The entry includes engine-owned scale limits for one-third of original shares at
+1R and one-third at +2R. The remaining shares are the runner. There are no adds,
arms, re-entries, or discretionary target changes.

## Management and exit

The engine resolves a protective stop and active scale limits before each new
bar-close decision.

Exit the whole remaining position on the first matching rule:

1. mandatory flat at or after 15:55 ET;
2. during the first two bars after entry, close below the saved five-minute
   `break_level`;
3. exit-pressure score at least 70; or
4. completed red five-minute candle closes below the most recent completed green
   five-minute candle's low.

Exit-pressure score, capped at 100:

- +35 when the current minute closes below VWAP;
- up to +25 from the single highest-quality current bearish pattern;
- +20 for a completed red five-minute candle with `volume_ratio >= 1.5`;
- +10 after two consecutive negative one-minute MACD histograms;
- +10 after five bars without a post-entry high when price remains below
  `avg_entry + 0.25 × initial_stop_distance`.

A bearish pattern alone cannot reach the exit threshold. If early price reaches
`avg_entry + min($0.10, initial_stop_distance / 3)` and still closes at or above
average entry during bar one or two, raise the stop to break-even. Also raise the
stop to break-even after the first engine scale fill. Never loosen a stop.

If an `EXIT_CLOSE` is volume-capped and leaves shares, the policy repeats the exit
intent until flat. Once flat, it stands down for the rest of the stream.

## Audit contract

Every decision persists:

- `policy_id` and `decision_source`;
- score total and exact component values when scored; and
- machine-readable reason codes.

Batch audit re-derives the entire decision sequence from the published sealed
stream using the session's frozen execution configuration and requires exact JSON
equality. The runner contract hashes the policy implementation and freezes its
numeric specification.
