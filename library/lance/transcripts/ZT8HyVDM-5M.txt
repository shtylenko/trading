In-play stocks are tickers that are active because of one of three reasons: a news catalyst, a major technical pattern, or meaningful volatility like in fresh IPOs. These are not your everyday slow-moving stocks. These are stocks where something is happening, where traders are battling, and where markets are trying to find the right price for the stock. Compared to regular stocks, in-play stocks offer clearer levels, bigger ranges, more volume, and more opportunities to capture edge. That's why I call them broken slot machines because, unlike the rest of the casino, these actually give the player a chance to beat the house.
