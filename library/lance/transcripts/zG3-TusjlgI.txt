Title: How to Be a Data-Driven Trader #trading #tradingstrategies #tradingplaybook
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=zG3-TusjlgI
======================================================================

Most traders are not data-driven. They don't know what playbooks are making them the most and the least money. They don't know the stats of each of their trades. They don't know the analytics for each of their months. They remember the one time they get chopped up and assume the setup doesn't work. They remember one big winner and assume they found the holy grail. They let recency bias build their strategy. But trading at its essence is a math game and we always want to be pushing ourselves towards making the most optimal data-driven decisions. Your job is to answer questions with evidence. What is the expectancy of each of your setups? What's the distribution of the trade outcomes? Under what conditions do your strategies perform best? And are you profitable because of a repeatable edge or because of a couple outlier trades? This is why journaling matters and tracking your data matters so much. When trying to find what constraint will offer the most upside to your trading, you need to utilize data to justify that decision.
