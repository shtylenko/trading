I talk about this all the time because it's the fuel that keeps you going. There are a lot of reasons people get into trading. Maybe they love challenges. Maybe they want to make money. Maybe they want to be their own boss or escape a job they hate. And honestly, there's no right or wrong reason. But this game is brutal. It never gets easy. Even at the top, I still have losing months. I still have days that make me want to quit. So, whatever your reason is, it has to be strong enough to carry you through all of it. If your why isn't powerful, if you're not emotionally invested in pushing through the pain, you're going to quit. Everyone does eventually. Unless they have something deep and real pulling them forward.
