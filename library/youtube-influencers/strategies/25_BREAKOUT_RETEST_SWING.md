# Breakout-Retest Swing Strategy
## Used by: Schwab Coaching (Connie Hill)

### Overview
Highest R:R swing setup. Stock breaks above a long consolidation on volume, then pulls back to test it. Enter on the retest hold. The retest filters ~40% of false breakouts.

### Why It Works
- ~40% of breakouts fail (bull traps)
- Retest filters out the failures
- Result: 45-55% win rate with 2.5-3:1 R/R
- Failed retest = -1R loss = built into system

### Consolidation Criteria
- 3-8+ weeks minimum
- Range narrows over time (contracting)
- 2-3 distinct touches of horizontal resistance
- Volume contracts during consolidation
- Clean horizontal level (not diagonal)

### Breakout Requirements
1. **Close above resistance** (intraday spike not enough)
2. **Volume 1.5-2x** average of consolidation
3. **Wide-range candle** (not small)
4. Level at upper end of range (not middle)

### Retest Entry (The Key)
**Sequence:**
1. Breakout fires (per criteria)
2. Within 1-5 days, price pulls back to the broken level
3. **Lower volume** on retest than on breakout
4. **Hold candle:** Bullish candle that touches/wicks below the level, then closes above
5. **Entry:** Next day open, or break above hold candle's high

### Stop Loss
- Below hold candle low
- Below the broken level (slightly wider)
- Typical: 3-7% below entry

### Target (Measured Move)
- Consolidation height projected upward from breakout level
- Example: Base height = $10, breakout at $100 → target = $110
- R/R: 2.5-3:1

### Important Stats
- ~30% of valid breakouts RUN without retesting (don't chase)
- If no retest in 5-7 days, setup expired
- Failed retest = -1R (part of the system)
- Counter-trend breakouts (vs weekly trend) fail often

### Risk Management
- Risk 1% of account per trade
- Structure-based stop (below the broken level)
- 3-5 positions max

### Adapting for $25-100k
- SPY/QQQ at key breakout zones
- Individual stocks: $10-200, good volume
- Best on daily chart
