This is the part most people overlook. Shitcoins still obey the same technical patterns as everything else. As I've long preached, technical analysis is like fractals. The same patterns exist across all time frames in all markets, regardless of product. That means I can trade panics, breakouts, or my famous bouncy ball pattern in chitcoins, the same way I trade them in stocks. The price action doesn't care what the ticker is, and neither should you. I don't care how new a coin is or how small the market cap is. As long as there is volatility, volume, and a chart setup in my playbook, I'm going to be open to taking that trade. Let me give you a few examples. Trumpcoin. I had a friend make over a million dollars by getting in fast on the launch. He recognized that in the current post-election euphoria, the meme factor was off the charts and the coin would moon, and it did. Then there's the Elon tweet. He posts a picture of a dog barking at the moon and sends Dogecoin up 40%. Yes, that's insanely stupid. I agree. But our job as traders is not to judge.
