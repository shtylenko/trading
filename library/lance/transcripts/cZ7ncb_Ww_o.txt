And real traders hate this stuff. Which is why you might be shocked to know that I think crypto and even shitcoins can offer some incredible trading opportunities. And I'm going to explain why that I, Lance Brightstein, an 8 figureure P&L per year trader that has spent over a decade trading on Wall Street, encourages you to be open-minded to trading shitcoins. I'm not talking about Bitcoin. I am talking about Dogecoin, Trumpcoin, and the random crypto projects that pump and dump. Think of them as penny stocks on steroids. if the stories were mixed with delusion and Twitter. These can have market caps in the tens of millions all the way up to the billions. So why do they make great trading opportunities? First, shitcoins offer inefficient markets with far less sophisticated players in them. You're not competing against Wall Street's best traders who are armed with research teams, data feeds, and billions in capital. Second, because of that, it's easier to develop anformational edge. You can anticipate what headlines or trends are going to matter before they hit the mainstream. You're not late to the party because half the room doesn't even know there's a party yet. While headlines on stocks are often priced into the share price within minutes or even seconds, it can take hours or even days for the full impact of news to be priced in on these less followed markets. So, the third reason why shitcoins are great trading opportunities is because there are natural barriers to entry. A lot of big firms don't trust the exchanges. They don't want the counterparty risk.
