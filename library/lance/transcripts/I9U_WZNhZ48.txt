Title: One of My Worst Trading Losses #trading #stocks #tradingpsychology
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=I9U_WZNhZ48
======================================================================

A boutique short report came out by a group called Rota Fortuna on a stock ticker FPI. FPI is a boring farmland REIT, and the short report by Rota Fortuna argued that the company was massively overvalued, financially weak, and potentially misleading investors about its true financial condition. Because FPI just owns land, I started to play analyst, dismissing the price action and thinking there's no way that a simple farmland REIT could drop so much on one dumb boutique short report. This was faulty logic and poor logic, which led me to fight the move lower, and that ended up being catastrophic for me. The loss at the time wasn't anything so, so huge. I think it was maybe just 150 grand, but I was just so tired of fighting the trend given how much of my trading was focused on mean reversion. Going home from that loss after already a challenging year, I felt defeated and that I needed a change.
