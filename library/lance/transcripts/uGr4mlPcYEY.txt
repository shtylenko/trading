Is trading a sustainable long-term career? We all know how emotionally and psychologically difficult trading can be. It's a very, very tough job. So, I think it's reasonable to ask this, like, are there traders out there that can do this for decades? And I think it comes down to this. You absolutely can do it as long as you have a strong why. That why really needs to be a passion and love for the game. this just for the money or some external measure of success. Once you achieve that, you're not going to be able to keep on going. The other thing that I think is important, you also need balance and an obsession with the process. As long as you have other important things to you outside of trading and as long as you focus on the process, there's no reason why you can't have a multi-deade career. And I've seen many traders do just that.
