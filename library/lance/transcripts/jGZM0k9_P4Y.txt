The candle gives you everything as long as you know where to look. If the candlestick looks like this, buyers are stepping back in. This [music] one looks similar, but it's flipped. If you see a big green candle that completely covers a smaller red one, that's showing bullish strength. The opposite is a bearish engulfing. A big red candle eats a small green one. Finally, the shooting star. Small body, long top wicks. That shows a failed rally. Buyers pushed it up. Sellers slammed it back down. Most traders, guess what a chart is saying?
