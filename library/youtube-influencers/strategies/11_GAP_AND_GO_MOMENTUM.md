# Gap and Go Momentum Strategy
## Used by: Ross Cameron (Warrior Trading) — $583 to $10M

### Overview
Buy stocks that **gap up significantly** on a positive catalyst and **continue higher** with strong volume. Ross Cameron turned $583 into $10M+ primarily using this approach. Also used by Larry Jones (Stock Up), Alex Temiz.

### Core Concept
When a stock gaps up on a catalyst (earnings beat, FDA approval, major contract), institutional buyers rush in. If volume is strong and price holds above the gap level and VWAP, the momentum can continue for hours to days.

### Key Catalyst Types
1. **Earnings beat** with raised guidance (most reliable)
2. **FDA approval** (biotech)
3. **Major contract wins**
4. **Analyst upgrade** from major firm
5. **Sector news** (competitor bought out, regulation change)

### Pre-Market Screening
- Gap size: **5-20%** (too small = weak, too large = might fade)
- Pre-market volume: at least 50% of average daily volume
- Float: Lower float = more explosive moves
- News catalyst must be **verifiable and meaningful**

### Volume Requirements
- First 10-30 minutes: Should see **average daily volume** traded
- This confirms institutional participation
- Volume surge on the breakout > volume at the open

### Entry Methods

**1. VWAP Hold Entry**
- Stock gaps up and holds above VWAP
- If stock stays above VWAP for first 5-10 minutes = bullish
- Enter when price shows strength above VWAP

**2. Opening Range Breakout**
- Wait for first 5-minute candle to establish range
- Enter when price breaks above the opening range high
- Stop: below opening range low

**3. Pre-Market High Breakout**
- Mark the pre-market high
- Enter when price breaks above it with volume
- Indicates continuation of momentum

### Critical Rules
**Alex Temiz 30% Rule:** If price is above VWAP (showing strength), use max 30% of intended position size. Only add full size after VWAP breaks in your direction or a "death candle" confirms.

**Zombie Rule (Alex Temiz):** Stop trading by **10:30 AM EST**. After this, momentum fades and stocks get choppy. This rule saved $1,000/day in losses.

### Stop Loss Placement
- **Tight:** Below VWAP
- **Standard:** Below opening range low
- **Wide:** Below the gap fill level
- **Time stop:** Exit by 10:30 AM if trade hasn't developed

### Profit Targets
- **Measured move:** Gap size + intraday momentum
- **Prior resistance:** Next technical level
- **Time-based:** Most gap-and-go moves peak by 10:30-11:00 AM
- Scale out: 50% at first target, trail remainder

### Risk Management
- Risk 1-2% of account per day, not per trade
- Small position sizing (gappers are volatile)
- Never add to a losing position
- No overnight holds on gap trades

### Adapting for $25-100k Account
- Focus on stocks $10-100/share with catalysts
- Use 100-500 share positions
- Pre-screen night before for earnings gappers
- Only trade first 1-2 hours of market open

### Common Mistakes
1. Chasing stocks already up 30%+ before entry
2. Ignoring pre-market volume
3. Trading against VWAP direction
4. Holding past 10:30 AM when momentum fades
5. Adding to losers (averaging down on gappers)
