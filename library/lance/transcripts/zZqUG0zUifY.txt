Circling back to another frequently asked question. Are you sure I shouldn't have multiple goals at once? Many people try this and explaining the psychology behind this is way beyond my intelligence. But the research backs up the fact that people do much better with one goal at a time. If you do one goal at a time, maybe each goal takes you between three and four weeks before you move on. If I were to try and do three goals at once, four or five months later, you've done none of them to the point that you would have otherwise. Keep in mind, some goals can be incredibly difficult to reprogram, especially under the pressure of real-time trading, which can be very fast-paced.
