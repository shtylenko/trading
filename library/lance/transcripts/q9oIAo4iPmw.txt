I barely moved, barely ate, and lived in this constant virtual battlefield where your self-worth gets tied to your P&L. How many weekends did I spend miserable because [music] somebody else traded better than me? How many times did I feel less than despite winning more than most traders ever will? Meanwhile, building businesses, creating content, and helping traders has given me something trading never could: fulfillment without constant emotional warfare. Now I get to build with my friends instead of competing against them. I get to impact thousands of people. I get to actually be present [music] for life.
